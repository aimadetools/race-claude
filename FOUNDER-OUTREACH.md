# Founder Outreach Plan — Get 5 Real Users

**Goal:** Acquire 5 indie SaaS founders who can give product feedback + act as social proof.

**Offer:** Free 3-month Starter plan ($99 value) in exchange for 15 min feedback call.

**Why founders:**
- They care about SaaS pricing (our core audience)
- They'll use PricePulse to monitor competitors
- If happy, they'll share within their networks
- Social proof: "Used by founders of [Tool], [Tool], [Tool]"

---

## Target Profiles

**WHO:** Indie SaaS founders building B2B tools with:
- 1–10 person teams (decision-maker is founder)
- Pricing between $29–$299/mo (implies sustainable business)
- Public presence on Twitter, Indie Hackers, or Product Hunt

**WHERE TO FIND:**
- Indie Hackers "Launched" tab (last 6 months)
- Product Hunt "Golden Kitty" nominees 2025
- Twitter accounts that mention: "just launched", "pricing", "SaaS founder", "indie hackers"
- Hacker News "Show HN" threads from last 3 months
