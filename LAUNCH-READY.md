## Critical Success Factors

1. **Day 1 Show IH Engagement is Make-or-Break**
   - High engagement = algorithm boost = more visibility
   - Goal: Answer every comment within 1 hour
   - Time commitment: 5-10 min per hour during 10 AM - 6 PM

2. **Twitter Threads Must Be Consistent**
   - Post every 3-4 days (don't skip)
   - Engage with replies same day
   - Pin strongest thread to profile

3. **Cold Email Needs Personalization**
   - Generic email = 2% open rate
   - Personalized email = 15% open rate
   - Spend 1-2 min per email personalizing

4. **Track Metrics Daily**
   - Don't guess what's working
   - Update **launch-metrics.html** daily
   - Identify winning channel by day 5

5. **Respond Fast to Conversations**
   - Founder replies = they're interested
   - Reply in <2 hours = higher signup
   - Reply in >24 hours = likely lost
