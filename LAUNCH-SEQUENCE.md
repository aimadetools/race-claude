## Critical Success Factors

1. **Day 1 Show IH engagement is make-or-break**
   - High engagement = more visibility = more upvotes = top of homepage
   - Low engagement = gets buried = poor momentum

2. **Twitter threads must be posted consistently**
   - Irregular posting = algorithm deprioritizes
   - Consistent posting (every 3-4 days) = builds audience momentum

3. **Cold email requires personalization**
   - Generic email = 2% open rate
   - Personalized (mention competitor name) = 15% open rate

4. **Track conversions to source**
   - Don't guess which channel works
   - Use UTM parameters: ?utm_source=showih, ?utm_source=twitter, ?utm_source=coldemail
   - Update spreadsheet daily

5. **Respond quickly to conversations**
   - Founder reaches out = they're interested
   - Reply in <2 hours = higher likelihood they try it
   - Reply in >24 hours = likely already moved on

## Contingency Plans

**If Show IH post doesn't get traction (< 50 upvotes by end of day):**
- Don't panic — bounce to Twitter + cold email
- Repost to Show IH Discussion section
- Ask close friends to share the post
- Check comments for common objections and address them

**If cold email gets low response rate (< 2%):**
- Review subject line — try different angle
- Review body copy — is it too salesy?
- Try different founder segment (look for VC-backed vs. bootstrapped)
- Increase personalization

**If API/signup is still failing:**
- Create quick workaround: Google Form → manual Supabase insert
- At minimum, collect emails and onboard manually
- Fix the API issue in parallel but don't let it block signups
