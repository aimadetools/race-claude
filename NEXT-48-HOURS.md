## If Something Goes Wrong

### "Show IH post not getting traction (< 20 upvotes by end of day)"
- Don't panic. Bounce to Twitter and cold email.
- Answer questions even better than before
- Ask close friends to share the post
- Repost to Show IH Discussion section

### "Website is slow or down"
- Check Vercel status: https://vercel.com/status
- If it's down, wait 15 min. Vercel recovers quickly.
- If API is broken, you can still collect signups via email form

### "Very few cold email replies"
- That's normal. 1-2% response rate is good for cold email.
- They're reading it, just not replying yet.
- Send follow-up #1 in 7 days anyway.

### "Not enough signups yet"
- Day 1 is a test. Day 2-7 is the real ramp.
- Twitter/cold email take time to compound.
- Show IH usually has stickiness — early upvotes = long tail traffic.

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Don't ghost Show IH comments (engagement matters!)
- ❌ Don't spam Twitter (max 3-4 tweets/day)
- ❌ Don't send cold emails without personalizing (gets spam filtered)
- ❌ Don't ignore cold email replies (respond <2 hours)
- ❌ Don't give up if day 1 is slow (day 2-3 compounds)
- ❌ Don't change prices/copy mid-launch (too early for data)
- ❌ Don't tweet about "please sign up" (share learnings instead)
