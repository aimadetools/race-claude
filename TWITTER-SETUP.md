- Second line: Who built it (founder credibility)
- URL: Direct link to product

**Limit:** 160 characters max (this is 127 — you have room for more)

- Optional but good for context

## Step 3: Configure Privacy Settings (2 min)

Click Settings and privacy → Privacy and safety

**Recommended settings:**
- [ ] **Allow anyone to message you** (YES — for cold email replies, DMs matter)
- [ ] **Allow message requests from anyone** (YES)
- [ ] **Who can reply to your tweets** (Everyone is fine)
- [ ] **Who can tag you in photos** (Everyone)
- [ ] **Who can find you by email/phone** (Everyone)

**Turn ON:**
- [ ] **Protect your Tweets** (NO — you want public reach)
- [ ] **Show analytics** (YES — you'll want to see engagement)

## Step 8: Schedule Your First Thread (Optional)

Twitter allows scheduling tweets up to 12 months in advance.

**To schedule:**
1. Click Compose
2. Paste thread text
3. Click "Schedule" (calendar icon) instead of Tweet
4. Set date/time: Tomorrow 9 AM ET or today 6 PM ET
5. Click "Schedule Tweet"

**Best posting times:**
- 9 AM - 12 PM ET (morning coffee, people checking Twitter)

## Troubleshooting

**Q: My account is shadowbanned or tweets aren't getting impressions**
A: Twitter sometimes flags new accounts. Wait 24-48 hours. If still low engagement, check:
- Are your tweets using too many hashtags? (limit to 2-3)
- Are you using trending hashtags you don't belong in? (stay relevant)
- Are you tweeting too often? (don't spam — max 3-4 tweets/day)

**Q: Should I pay for Twitter Blue (verification)?**
A: Not necessary. Twitter Blue (paid) is for existing accounts wanting verification. New accounts don't benefit much. Skip it for now.

**Q: How do I get more followers before launch?**
A: Engage genuinely:
- Reply thoughtfully to other founders' tweets (1-2 per day)
- Retweet useful content (2-3 per day)
- Don't ask for follows; let them come naturally
- Share your story/learnings first, pitch product second

**Q: Can I use automated posting tools?**
A: Yes, but carefully:
- Buffer, Later, SocialFlow all work with Twitter
- Schedule your 7 threads in advance if you want
- BUT: Show up for replies/engagement in real-time (don't auto-reply)
- Readers can tell if you're not actually there

## Pro Tips for Success

1. **Engage with replies immediately**
   - Set a phone reminder: Reply to comments within 1 hour
   - Thank people for sharing, answer technical questions
   - This signals to Twitter's algorithm that your tweets are generating good conversation

2. **Screenshot the demo page for visual tweets**
   - A tweet with a screenshot gets 50% more engagement than text-only
   - Example: "Here's what a real pricing change looks like" + demo screenshot

3. **Share quotes that resonate**
   - People retweet good quotes more than straight pitches
   - Thread 1 example: "Every Friday I'd open 15 tabs to check if anything changed. There had to be a better way."

4. **Tag relevant people (but don't spam)**
   - @indiehackers is good for every launch tweet
   - Tag @tjholowaychuk or other founder accounts only if relevant (not every tweet)
   - Spam tags = account shadow-ban

5. **Use simple language**
   - "Monitors" not "orchestrates intelligent surveillance"
   - "Alerts you" not "notifies you asynchronously"
   - Short sentences beat long run-ons

6. **Avoid these mistakes**
   - Don't be salesy ("BUY NOW" vs. "try free")
   - Don't bad-mouth competitors (critique, not attack)
   - Don't post drunk or angry (sleep on controversial takes)
   - Don't engage with bots or trolls (ignore them)
