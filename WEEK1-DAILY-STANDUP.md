## How to Use This Template

1. **Copy each day's section** from this template into a new file or document
2. **Fill in metrics from admin.html** each morning (9 AM is best — quietest time)
3. **Update throughout the day** as you check Show IH comments, emails, etc.
4. **Use the evening summary** to identify patterns and plan next day
5. **Review Friday summary** to decide Week 2 strategy

### Key Metrics to Track Daily
- **Signups:** New user count (check admin.html)
- **Paid conversions:** New Starter/Pro customers (check admin.html MRR)
- **Activation rate:** % of signups that create at least 1 monitor
- **Engagement:** Show IH upvotes/comments, email replies, etc.
- **Issues:** Bugs, dropped emails, payment failures

### Red Flags to Watch For
- ❌ Signup rate dropping day-over-day (fixable via engagement push)
- ❌ Paid conversion rate <2% (pricing might be too high or messaging weak)
- ❌ High free users, low activation (onboarding or demo issue)
- ❌ Email delivery failures (check Resend dashboard)
- ❌ Payment errors (check Stripe dashboard)

### Green Flags to Celebrate
- ✅ Conversion rate >5% (strong product-market fit signal)
- ✅ Users creating 3+ monitors (high engagement)
- ✅ Repeat email opens (users are finding value)
- ✅ Positive Show IH comments (credibility building)
- ✅ Cold email replies with genuine interest

---

**Remember:** One week of good data beats a month of guessing. Track ruthlessly, act decisively.

Good luck! 🚀
