About

Built for founders who hate surprises

PricePulse exists because every SaaS founder has had the same experience: finding out a competitor changed their pricing weeks later, from a customer.

Why this exists

Competitive pricing intelligence is a real problem. Crayon, Klue, and Kompyte solve it brilliantly — for enterprise teams with $1,500/month to spend. For the indie founder, the solo SaaS builder, the 3-person startup that just crossed $5K MRR? The answer has always been "check manually."

PricePulse fills that gap. A $19/month tool that does one thing well: tells you the moment a competitor touches their pricing page.

Nothing more, nothing less. No bloated competitive intelligence platform. No "market research" dashboards. Just: something changed, here's what, here's the diff.

What we're building toward

Right now, PricePulse monitors pricing pages and sends diffs. That's the core product and it already solves the main problem.

In the coming weeks, we're adding:

  • Slack integration (alerts where your team already lives)
  • Price trend charts (visualize competitor pricing over time)
  • Competitor comparison dashboard (see all changes at a glance)
  • Zapier + API (connect to your CRM or sales workflow)

The competition

We know — it's a bit meta for a competitive intelligence tool to talk about its own competition. Here's how we see it:

Crayon, Klue, Kompyte: Great tools. $500-2000/month. Not for indie founders.

Visualping, Wachete: Generic website change monitors. Don't understand pricing pages, don't filter noise, don't extract meaningful changes.

Manual monitoring: Still what 90% of founders do. We're replacing the "check competitor pricing every Monday morning" ritual.

PricePulse is purpose-built for pricing intelligence at an indie-friendly price point. There's nobody else in this exact spot.
