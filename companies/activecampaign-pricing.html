ActiveCampaign Pricing 2026

ActiveCampaign is an all-in-one email marketing, CRM, and sales automation platform. Start at $15/mo with email and basic automation, scale to $150+/mo for advanced features. No overage fees for exceeding contact limits.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Lite: $15/mo (500 contacts)
  • Plus: $50/mo (1,500 contacts)
  • Professional: $150/mo (10K+ contacts)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing
  • All tiers include: Email, CRM, automation, forms, integrations
  • No overage fees: Upgrade plan if you exceed contacts

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Start with Lite ($15/mo): Perfect for testing email + CRM before scaling. No long-term contract required.
  • Use contact segmentation: Inactive or cold contacts still count toward limits. Regularly clean old contacts to stay within tier.
  • Leverage automation: Build workflows to reduce manual work and improve ROI on email marketing.
  • Combine email + CRM: ActiveCampaign bundles both, so you don't need separate tools (unlike HubSpot).
  • Annual billing discount: Pay annually for 10-20% discount vs monthly billing.
