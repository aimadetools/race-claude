Warning: Adobe Annual Plan Cancellation Fees

If you cancel an annual-paid-monthly plan before the year is up, Adobe charges 50% of your remaining contract value. On the All Apps plan at $59.99/month, canceling at month 6 costs $179.97 in fees. This was the subject of a 2024 FTC lawsuit — Adobe agreed to make these fees more visible but they still apply.

Adobe Creative Cloud Pricing History (2012–2026)

FTC lawsuit over cancellation fees; Adobe settles for $90M 2024
The FTC sued Adobe in June 2024 alleging that Adobe enrolled users in annual subscriptions without adequately disclosing the early termination fees. Adobe agreed to a $90 million settlement. As part of the settlement, Adobe made cancellation fees more prominent during checkout — but did not eliminate them. The lawsuit drew widespread attention to Adobe's practice of burying the 50% early cancellation fee deep in checkout flows.
Result:$90M FTC settlement; fees disclosed more prominently
All Apps (Individual) raised from $54.99 to $59.99/mo; Teams from $79.99 to $89.99/seat May 2023
Adobe raised Creative Cloud All Apps Individual by $5/month (9% increase) and Teams by $10/seat/month (12.5% increase). Adobe cited "significant investment in AI and new features including Adobe Firefly generative AI." The price increase landed while Adobe was also attempting (and ultimately failing) to acquire Figma for $20B — the deal was abandoned after EU/UK regulatory opposition in late 2023.
All Apps (Individual):$54.99/mo→$59.99/mo (+9%)
All Apps (Teams):$79.99/seat/mo→$89.99/seat/mo (+12.5%)
Storage limits unchanged; Creative Cloud Express added to all plans 2022
Adobe added Creative Cloud Express (now Adobe Express) to all paid plans — effectively bundling a Canva competitor into the suite. No price change accompanied this addition, but it was used to justify the 2023 price increase ("more value per subscription"). Storage limits remained at 100GB for individual and 1TB for Teams, despite competitors offering more.
Adobe Express:Added to all plans without price increase
Adobe moves to subscription-only; perpetual licenses discontinued 2013
Adobe Creative Suite 6 (2012) was the last version sold as a perpetual license. With CS7, Adobe transitioned entirely to the subscription Creative Cloud model. The original CC pricing started at $49.99/month for all apps. This transition was deeply controversial — designers who owned CS6 perpetual licenses could no longer purchase future updates without subscribing. Adobe lost significant market share to Affinity Suite in the following years.
Business model:Perpetual license ($1,300+ one-time)→Subscription-only ($49.99/mo)

Adobe CC vs Alternatives: Where Teams Are Moving

Adobe's 2023 price increase and the FTC cancellation fee lawsuit accelerated a trend of teams evaluating alternatives. The main beneficiaries:

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Adobe Creative Cloud cost per month in 2026?

Adobe Creative Cloud All Apps costs $59.99/month on an annual plan (billed monthly) or $719.88/year if paid upfront annually. Month-to-month pricing (no commitment) is $89.99/month. The Photography Plan (Lightroom + Photoshop only) is $9.99/month annual or $19.99/month on month-to-month.

What happens if I cancel Adobe Creative Cloud early?

If you cancel an annual plan paid monthly before the end of your contract year, Adobe charges 50% of your remaining balance as an early termination fee. If you're 4 months into a 12-month plan at $59.99/month, canceling costs you 50% × (8 × $59.99) = $239.96. Month-to-month plans have no cancellation fee — they just cost 50% more per month.

Is there a cheaper alternative to Adobe Creative Cloud?

Yes. For professional designers: Affinity Suite ($69.99 one-time or $16.99/year) covers most Photoshop/Illustrator/InDesign workflows. For UI/UX teams: Figma ($15/seat/month). For content creation: Canva Pro ($15/user/month). For photographers: Capture One ($24/month) or Lightroom standalone ($9.99/month). None of these individually replicate the full Adobe suite, but most workflows only need 1-2 tools.

Does Adobe Creative Cloud include Acrobat Pro?

The All Apps plan includes Acrobat Pro for PDF editing. Acrobat Pro is also available as a standalone at $19.99/month (annual). This is often overlooked — teams paying for Acrobat Pro separately while also subscribing to All Apps are double-paying for this feature.
