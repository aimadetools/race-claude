Ahrefs Pricing History (2024–2026)

Ahrefs historically avoided frequent pricing changes. The 2026 restructure was its most significant in several years:

Plan restructure with price increases across all tiers March 2026
Ahrefs raised prices on all three core plans by $30–$50/month. The company simultaneously expanded crawl limits and keyword data freshness, framing the increase as a value upgrade. Monthly subscribers were migrated immediately; annual subscribers at next renewal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial for Ahrefs in 2026?

Ahrefs removed its 7-day paid trial in 2022 and replaced it with Ahrefs Free Tools — a suite of limited-access tools. There is no traditional free trial. Annual plans provide approximately 2 months free compared to monthly billing. Some agencies offer trial access via team accounts.

What's the cheapest way to use Ahrefs in 2026?

The Lite plan at $108/month (billed annually) is the entry point. For individuals who only need occasional research, Ahrefs offers a $29 "pay per report" option for some tools. The free tools suite covers basic keyword and backlink lookups with daily limits.

How does Ahrefs compare to Semrush on price?

After the March 2026 increase, Ahrefs Lite ($129/mo) is more expensive than Semrush Pro ($119/mo). Ahrefs Standard ($249/mo) is comparable to Semrush Guru ($229/mo). Semrush includes more tools (social, content, PPC) while Ahrefs is considered more accurate for backlink data and keyword difficulty scoring.

Can I share one Ahrefs account with my team?

The Lite plan is single-user only. Standard allows additional seats at $50/seat/month. Advanced includes 3 seats. For teams of 4+, Enterprise pricing is typically more cost-effective than adding individual seats to Standard.

Does Ahrefs give discounts to agencies?

Ahrefs doesn't publish an agency discount program. High-volume users typically negotiate Enterprise rates directly. Some agencies use the Advanced or Enterprise plans and distribute access across their team rather than adding per-seat costs.
