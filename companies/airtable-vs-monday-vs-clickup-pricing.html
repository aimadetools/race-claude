When to Choose Each Tool

✓ Choose ClickUp if:

  • Budget is tight (cheapest per-seat pricing at any team size)
  • You need unlimited automations and time tracking
  • You want an all-in-one solution (project mgmt + docs + chat)
  • You value unlimited API calls and integrations

Best for: Startups, budget-conscious teams, product teams

✓ Choose Monday if:

  • You want a visual, workflow-focused tool
  • You need a balance between features and cost
  • Your team loves kanban/timeline views
  • You want native integrations and good mobile experience

Best for: Marketing teams, operations, cross-functional projects

✓ Choose Airtable if:

  • You need database-first approach (fields, relations, rollups)
  • You're building custom workflows with Automations + Zapier
  • You value spreadsheet-like interface with database power
  • You need sophisticated field types (lookups, rollups, formulas)

Best for: Product managers, analysts, operations, customer success
