When to Upgrade from Free to Growth

Upgrade if:

  • You hit 100M events/year consistently (100K+ users)
  • You need real-time data (free tier is delayed)
  • You want advanced features: custom events, audience sync, predictive analytics
  • Your team has more than 3 analysts (free tier has limited seats)

Stay on free if:

  • You have < 100K users (annual events < 100M)
  • You only need basic retention/funnel reports
  • Budget is tight (use Google Analytics 4 instead)
  • You're still validating product-market fit

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I estimate my event volume before signing up?

Yes. Use Amplitude's event calculator or estimate: (monthly users) × (average events per user per day) × 365.

Does Amplitude have a way to sample events to reduce costs?

Yes. Use Amplitude's sampling feature to track 100% of high-value users and 10% of others, reducing event volume.

Is Amplitude better than Google Analytics?

For product teams (retention, cohorts, funnels): Yes. For marketing/website analytics: Google Analytics is better and cheaper.

Can I export data from Amplitude?

Yes. Export raw events via API or use Amplitude's integrations (Segment, S3, Snowflake) to sync data to your data warehouse.
