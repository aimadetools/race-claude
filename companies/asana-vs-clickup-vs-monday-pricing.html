When to Choose Each Platform

  • Prefer polished, professional interface
  • Budget: $1,619/year for 10 people
  • Strongest free tier (15 members)

Choose ClickUp If:

  • Want all-in-one platform (tasks + docs + chat)
  • Need maximum customization flexibility
  • Budget-conscious (26% cheaper than Asana)

  • Budget: $1,200/year for 10 people (cheapest)

Choose Monday.com If:

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I migrate from Asana to ClickUp?

Yes. Both tools support CSV exports/imports. ClickUp has strong migration guides and will help move your data. Plan 1-2 weeks for a full team migration with testing. Smaller teams can migrate in days.

Why did ClickUp raise prices 43%?

ClickUp raised Unlimited plan from $7 to $10/seat in February 2026 to fund product expansion (docs, AI features, whiteboards). Even after the increase, ClickUp remains cheaper than Asana ($13.49) and Monday ($12).

Which is best for Agile/Scrum teams?

All three support Agile workflows. Monday.com has the most intuitive sprint views (visual boards). ClickUp is most flexible for custom Agile setups. Asana supports sprints but less naturally than the other two.

Which integrates best with Slack?

All three have strong Slack integrations. ClickUp has the most native Slack features (task creation, updates, reminders directly in Slack). Monday has powerful workflow automation with Slack. Asana's Slack app is solid for notifications.

Does pricing include annual discounts?

Yes. Asana: ~10% annual discount. ClickUp: ~20% annual discount. Monday: ~20% annual discount. Annual billing typically saves 10-20% across all three platforms.
