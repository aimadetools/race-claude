How to Reduce Azure Costs

  • Reserved Instances: Commit to 1-3 years, save 30-72%. Lock in rates for stable workloads.
  • Spot VMs: Excess capacity at 70-90% discount. Good for batch jobs, non-critical workloads.
  • Auto-scaling: Scale down when traffic is low. Use Azure Autoscale for cost optimization.
  • Optimize storage: Move cold data to Archive tier ($0.002/GB). Use blob lifecycle policies.
  • Use PaaS over IaaS: App Service, Functions cheaper than managing VMs for many workloads.
  • Monitor continuously: Use Azure Cost Management + Billing to track and optimize spend.
