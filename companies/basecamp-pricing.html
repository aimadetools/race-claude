Basecamp Pricing 2026

Basecamp is an all-in-one project management platform with a simple, transparent pricing model: $99/month flat rate for unlimited users, projects, and features. Ideal for small to mid-size teams looking for an all-in-one solution without per-user costs.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free Trial: 30 days (no credit card required)
  • Basecamp: $99/month flat rate (unlimited users, projects, storage)
  • Annual Discount: ~20% off when paying yearly (~$950/year)
  • Setup fee: $0 (no setup costs)
  • Best for: Teams of all sizes, unlimited users, no per-user pricing

Basecamp Features (All Plans Include)

  • To-Do Lists: Assign tasks, set due dates, track progress
  • Message Boards: Team discussions, threaded conversations
  • File Sharing: Upload, organize, and comment on files
  • Schedule/Calendar: Milestones, deadlines, event scheduling
  • Real-Time Chat: Direct messaging and group chat
  • Automatic Check-Ins: Status updates from team members
  • Time Tracking: Built-in time tracking for logged hours
  • Document Collaboration: Write and edit documents together
  • Email Integration: Forward emails directly to projects
  • Mobile Apps: iOS and Android apps included

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Pay Annually for Discount: Save ~20% by paying for a full year upfront (~$950 instead of $1,188).
  • Try 30-Day Free Trial: Test Basecamp for a full month without any commitment. Great for evaluating if the flat-rate model works for your team.
  • Eliminate Per-User Costs: Unlike Asana or Monday, add unlimited users to Basecamp at no extra cost. Perfect for growing teams.
  • Combine with Other Tools: Basecamp integrates with Slack, Google Drive, and Zapier. Use integrations to avoid duplicate tool costs.
  • Leverage Templates: Use Basecamp's project templates to streamline setup and reduce training time.
