BigCommerce Pricing 2026

BigCommerce is an e-commerce platform with built-in marketing, multi-channel selling, and unlimited products. Pricing starts at $29/month and includes sales channels and marketing tools that other platforms charge extra for.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Standard: $29/month (unlimited products, 3 sales channels, email marketing)
  • Plus: $79/month (6 sales channels, Zapier, advanced analytics, 10 staff accounts)
  • Pro: $299/month (10 sales channels, priority support, advanced inventory, API)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (unlimited channels, custom solutions, dedicated support)
  • Transaction fees: $0 (unlike Squarespace or Etsy)
  • Best for: Mid-to-large stores needing multi-channel selling and built-in marketing

When to Choose BigCommerce

  • Multi-channel selling: Selling on Amazon, eBay, Facebook, Instagram simultaneously. Built-in sync is a major advantage.
  • No transaction fees: Higher revenue stores save money on transaction fees vs Shopify (2.9% + $0.30 on every order).
  • Unlimited products: Catalog with 10K+ SKUs runs better on BigCommerce than Shopify.
  • Built-in email marketing: No need for Klaviyo or Mailchimp integration costs.
  • B2B/wholesale: BigCommerce has B2B features (quotes, custom pricing by customer) on all plans.
