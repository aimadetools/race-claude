Brevo Pricing 2026

Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) is an email and SMS platform with unlimited emails on all plans, built-in transactional email support, and advanced automation—often cheaper than Mailchimp or ConvertKit at scale.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0 (300 contacts, unlimited emails, basic automations)
  • Essentials: $20/month (20K contacts, unlimited emails, SMS included, transactional email)
  • Business: $48/month (unlimited contacts, advanced segmentation, dedicated IP, priority support)
  • Premium: Custom pricing (white-label, dedicated account manager, API)
  • Key advantage: SMS included on all plans (Mailchimp charges extra)
  • Best for: Growing businesses needing email + SMS automation bundled together

When to Choose Brevo

  • You need email + SMS in one platform: Brevo's $20/mo Essentials includes both; Mailchimp charges $20+ extra for SMS
  • You send transactional emails: Order confirmations, password resets, alerts—all included in Brevo at no extra cost
  • Your list is growing beyond 5K contacts: Brevo stays at $20/mo up to 20K contacts; Mailchimp would cost more
  • You want unlimited email sends: All Brevo plans have unlimited emails; Mailchimp's free tier doesn't
  • You're scaling to 20K+ contacts: Business plan at $48/mo covers unlimited contacts and is the cheapest option at scale

When to Choose Alternatives

  • You're a creator/newsletter writer: ConvertKit ($25/mo) is purpose-built for newsletters with landing pages and subscriber management
  • You want landing pages: ConvertKit and Mailchimp have better landing page builders; Brevo's are more limited
  • You're starting completely free: ConvertKit free tier (1,000 subscribers) beats Brevo (300 contacts) and Mailchimp (500 contacts)

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Brevo and Sendinblue?

Brevo IS Sendinblue's new name (rebranded in 2022). All pricing and features are the same; just a name change. If you have an old Sendinblue account, it's now called Brevo.

Can I migrate from Mailchimp to Brevo?

Yes. Both tools support CSV exports/imports for contacts and emails. Plan 1-2 weeks for a full migration with testing. Brevo has helpful migration guides on their website.

Does Brevo offer annual discounts?

Yes, Brevo offers ~15-20% discount when paying annually instead of monthly. Essentials at $20/mo = $240/year, or save $36-48/year with annual billing.

How is Brevo's email deliverability?

Brevo has comparable deliverability rates to Mailchimp (95%+). Both have strong sender reputation management. Brevo's advantage: transactional email support (built-in), making it better for e-commerce.

What SMS volume is included in Brevo Essentials?

SMS pricing is separate—you pay per SMS sent (typically $0.03-0.05 per SMS depending on country). Brevo includes the platform; SMS credits are paid on top. Check their SMS pricing for your region.
