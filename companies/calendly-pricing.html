Calendly Pricing 2026

Calendly is a meeting scheduling automation platform that eliminates back-and-forth emails. With a free plan for basics and Premium at $14/month for individuals, Calendly is one of the most affordable scheduling solutions. Teams can collaborate with the Teams plan at $25/month per organizer.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0/month (basic scheduling, Calendly branding, 1 event type)
  • Premium: $14/month (annual) / $16/month (monthly) (unlimited event types, remove branding, API)
  • Teams: $25/month per organizer (shared calendars, group scheduling, min. 3 organizers)
  • Setup fee: $0 (no setup costs)
  • Best for: Sales professionals, consultants, customer-facing teams, meeting-heavy roles

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Annual Billing for Premium: Choose annual billing to save ~12% vs. monthly ($14/mo vs $16/mo).
  • Free Plan for Testing: Start with the free plan to evaluate. Calendly branding is acceptable for many solo users.
  • Premium Worth It: At $14/month annual, Premium is one of the cheapest scheduling tools available.
  • Teams at Scale: If you have 3+ organizers needing to coordinate, Teams at $25/user is competitive.
  • Use Integrations: Calendly integrates with Zapier, HubSpot, Slack, Google Workspace, Mailchimp. Avoid duplicate tools.
