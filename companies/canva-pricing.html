Canva Price Increase History

Canva has been profitable since 2021 and has used that position to gradually raise prices — particularly as it added AI features to justify higher tiers.

Pro Plan Price Increase — +15% August 2024
Canva raised Pro from $12.99/month to $15/month. Justified by new AI features including Magic Studio, Magic Write, and background remover enhancements. Existing subscribers received a 30-day notice via email.
$12.99/month→$15/month
Annual: $107.99/yr → $120/yr (+$12)
Teams Plan Restructured January 2024
Canva consolidated its "Canva for Teams" pricing, requiring a minimum of 2 seats ($20/month minimum). Previously, Teams plans were available from $13/user/month with no minimum seat requirement.
$13/seat, no minimum→$10/seat, 2 seat minimum
AI Features Moved to Paid Tiers October 2023
Canva launched Magic Studio, consolidating its AI tools under a premium umbrella. Many AI features previously available in the free tier (like limited Magic Write usage) were moved behind the Pro paywall or significantly rate-limited.
Pro Launch — No Price Change Since 2013–2022
Canva Pro launched at $12.99/month and held that price for nearly a decade. The stability was partly due to its freemium model and strategy of adding value via templates rather than feature gating. This ended in 2023–2024.

Is Canva Pro Worth It in 2026?

Canva Pro at $15/month ($120/year) is a strong value for individual creators and small business owners — especially compared to Adobe Express ($9.99/month) or buying individual stock image licenses. The break-even point is roughly 2–3 premium template purchases per month.

However, the Teams plan math changes quickly. At $10/seat/month with a 2-seat minimum, a 5-person team pays $600/year. Comparable tools like Adobe Express for Teams or Figma's Starter can be cheaper for design-heavy workflows.

The AI angle: Magic Studio features are only available on Pro and above. If you're using AI design tools regularly, the $15/month Pro plan is justified. If you're using Canva purely for occasional social posts, the free tier remains very capable.

Canva Pricing FAQ

Is Canva Free really free?
Yes, genuinely. Canva Free includes 250,000+ templates, 5GB cloud storage, and basic design tools. The main limitations are: no Brand Kit, no premium AI tools (Magic Studio), limited premium elements (most require payment or Pro), and no background remover. For casual use, the free tier is excellent.
Can I share Canva Pro with my team?
Not directly. Canva Pro is a single-user license. To collaborate, you need Canva for Teams ($10/seat/month, 2 seat minimum). If you share a Pro login, you risk account suspension and you won't get collaboration features like shared brand kits and team templates.
Does Canva offer a nonprofit or education discount?
Yes. Canva for Nonprofits and Canva for Education are both free for qualifying organizations. Schools and registered nonprofits can apply directly on the Canva website. Individual educators also qualify for free Pro access.
How often does Canva raise prices?
Canva held its Pro price at $12.99/month for nearly a decade before raising it in August 2024. Given its investment in AI features and enterprise push, analysts expect another increase within 12–18 months. What's the annual plan discount?
Canva Pro annual billing is $120/year vs $180/year monthly ($15 × 12), saving about 33%. Teams annual is $100/seat/year vs $120/seat/year monthly. The annual savings are significant enough that most regular users should commit to annual billing.
