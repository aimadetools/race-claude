Frequently Asked Questions

Is ClickUp or Asana cheaper?

ClickUp is dramatically cheaper. With annual billing, a 10-person team costs $50/year on ClickUp vs $1,318/year on Asana. ClickUp's pricing is one of its biggest advantages.

Why did ClickUp raise prices so much in 2026?

In February 2026, ClickUp increased prices by 43-58%. Unlimited went from $7 to $10 (43% increase), Business from $12 to $19 (58% increase). This was their largest increase ever and reflects competitive market pressures.

Is ClickUp easier than Asana?

No. Asana is easier to learn and get started with. ClickUp is more powerful and flexible but has a steeper learning curve. If ease of use is a priority, Asana is better.

Which is better for resource planning?

Asana is better. It has native portfolio management and capacity planning. ClickUp has basic resource planning but it's not as robust.

Can I use both ClickUp and Asana?

Technically yes, but it creates redundancy. Most teams choose one or the other. Given the huge price difference ($50 vs $1,318/year), choosing ClickUp and adding other specialized tools is often smarter.
