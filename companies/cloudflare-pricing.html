When to Upgrade from Free

The Free tier covers most sites indefinitely. Upgrade to Pro ($20/month) when:

  • You need advanced DDoS protection (Free tier covers common attacks)
  • You want priority support for production traffic
  • You want analytics and performance monitoring
  • You're running mission-critical applications

For 99% of projects, Free + Workers ($0.15/10M requests) is all you need.

Cloudflare vs Competitors

  • vs AWS CloudFront: Cloudflare is simpler and cheaper for small–medium sites. AWS CloudFront is more powerful for enterprise traffic patterns.
  • vs Akamai: Akamai is enterprise-only and much pricier. Cloudflare is self-serve and affordable.
  • vs Fastly: Both have edge computing. Fastly is more performant but pricier ($0.12/request vs $0.15). Cloudflare simpler API.
  • vs Netlify/Vercel: Those are full platforms. Cloudflare is CDN + edge computing. Both have free tiers but solve different problems.
