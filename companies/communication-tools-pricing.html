Communication Tool Pricing 2026

Slack raised prices twice in three years. Microsoft Teams is now free with M365 subscriptions millions of companies already pay for. Discord is free but targets communities. Zoom pivoted to an everything platform. Here's what you actually pay.

⚠️ Watch out: Teams is often "free" but hidden Microsoft Teams is included in Microsoft 365 Business Basic ($6/user/mo) — but many teams pay for it without realizing, since it's bundled. Meanwhile, Slack charges $7.25–$12.50/user/mo on top of your other tools.

Which Tool for Which Team
Slack
Best for: Tech startups, product teams, integrations-heavy workflows. Worth the cost if you live in it all day.
Microsoft Teams
Best for: Enterprises already on M365, companies needing deep Office integration, regulated industries.
Discord
Best for: Developer communities, gaming, creator communities, open-source projects. Basically free.
Zoom
Best for: Video-first teams, webinars, external client calls, teams without an existing video solution.
Google Meet
Best for: Teams already on Google Workspace — Meet is included, no extra cost.
Loom
Best for: Async-first teams, remote teams avoiding unnecessary meetings, code review videos.
