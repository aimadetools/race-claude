Confluence Pricing 2026 — Cloud Plans & User Cost

📊
Atlassian Cloud: Confluence moved to a per-user pricing model. Free tier supports 10 users; paid tiers start at $5.50/user/month (billed annually). Server tier was discontinued in February 2024.

Current Confluence Cloud Pricing (2026)

All Confluence Cloud plans include unlimited pages, spaces, and storage. Pricing is per user per month, with annual billing discounts:

When to Switch from Free to Paid

  • 10-user threshold: When you hit 10 users, Free tier caps out. You must move to Standard ($66/mo for 10 paid users).
  • Need advanced permissions: Standard (up to 50 users) handles most teams. Plus adds granular access controls.
  • Scaling to enterprise: Premium ($22/user) becomes necessary above 200 users for SLA, audit logs, and dedicated support.
  • Looking for cheaper alternatives: If budget is tight, try Notion ($10/workspace, covers unlimited team) or Coda ($10/user).
