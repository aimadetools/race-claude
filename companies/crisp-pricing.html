Crisp Pricing 2026

Crisp is an affordable all-in-one customer support platform combining live chat, email, ticketing, and CRM. With a generous free plan and a Starter tier at just €25/month (~$27 USD), Crisp is one of the cheapest customer support solutions for startups and small teams. Perfect alternative to expensive platforms like Intercom or Zendesk.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: €0/month (unlimited conversations, 1 agent, basic features)
  • Starter: €25/month (~$27 USD) (unlimited agents, advanced analytics, custom domain)
  • Pro: €95/month (~$103 USD) (advanced automation, priority support, premium integrations)
  • Setup fee: €0 (no setup costs)
  • Best for: Startups, small teams, cost-conscious customer support teams

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Start with Free: Crisp's free plan is surprisingly capable. Use it until you need multiple agents or advanced analytics.
  • Starter is a No-Brainer: At €25/month with unlimited agents, Crisp Starter is unbeatable vs. Zendesk ($245+) or Intercom ($500+).
  • Unlimited Agents: Unlike Zendesk ($49/agent), Crisp charges flat rate. Add 10 agents without extra cost.
  • Use Integrations: Crisp integrates with Slack, Zapier, HubSpot. Avoid redundant tools.
  • Consider Your Scale: For <5 agents, free is fine. 5-50 agents? Starter is best value. 50+ agents? Pro is still cheaper than alternatives.
