CRM Pricing 2026

HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zendesk, and Intercom are all competing for your customer data. CRM pricing is notoriously opaque — this hub exposes the real costs, hidden fees, and which CRM fits your stage and budget.

⚠️ CRM pricing is complex: Most CRMs charge per-seat AND per-contact AND per-feature add-on. Always calculate total cost (seats × contacts × add-ons) not just the headline price.

Which CRM for Which Stage?
HubSpot (Free → Starter)
Early-stage startups and SMBs who need a full marketing + sales stack. Free CRM is genuinely good. Upgrade when you need automation.
Pipedrive
Sales-first teams who live in their pipeline. Cleanest interface for deal tracking. Best value for $10–34/seat/mo range.
Salesforce
50+ person sales teams with complex processes, multi-product lines, or heavy customization needs. Overkill for most startups.
Zendesk
Support-heavy businesses (SaaS, e-commerce) with high ticket volume. Ticketing, help center, and agent routing in one platform.
Intercom
Product-led SaaS that needs in-app messaging, live chat, and customer success automation. Expensive but best for PLG motion.
