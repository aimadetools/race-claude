Datadog Pricing 2026 — Infrastructure, APM & Log Costs

📊
Datadog 2026: Per-host pricing model — each product (Infrastructure, APM, Logs) is billed separately. Infrastructure Pro starts at $15/host/month. Costs compound quickly when using multiple Datadog products. Bills can spike with log volume.

Datadog Infrastructure Monitoring Pricing

Datadog's core infrastructure product charges per host monitored. A "host" is any physical or virtual server, cloud instance, or container host running the Datadog agent.

Plan Per Host / Month Retention Best For
Free $0 1 day (5 hosts max) Evaluation, tiny projects
Pro $15/host 15 months Most production teams
Enterprise $23/host 15 months Orgs needing RBAC, SLOs, audit logs

Containers are billed at $0.002/container-hour (Pro) on top of host costs. Kubernetes nodes count as hosts.

Datadog APM (Application Performance Monitoring)

Plan Per Host / Month Included Spans Notes
APM Pro $31/host 1M indexed spans/host Includes distributed tracing
APM Enterprise $40/host 1M indexed spans/host Adds Data Streams, Error Tracking+

Additional indexed spans: $1.70/million. Ingested spans: $0.10/GB. APM is priced separately from infrastructure — you pay both if you use both.

Datadog Log Management Pricing

⚠️ Billing surprise: Log indexing (what you can search) costs significantly more than ingestion. Many teams get a bill 5–10x larger than expected because they didn't separate ingestion from indexing costs.
Product Price Notes
Log Ingestion $0.10/GB All logs ingested, not necessarily indexed
Log Indexing (Standard, 15 days) $1.70/million events Logs you can actively query
Log Indexing (Flex, 30 days) $0.05/million events/day Lower cost, higher latency queries
Log Archive (S3/GCS) Storage cost only Long-term storage with rehydration

Real-World Cost Scenarios

Small startup (5 hosts, infra only)
Infrastructure Pro (5 hosts) $75/month
No APM, no logs $0
Total monthly $75/month

Bare-bones infrastructure monitoring. Includes metrics, dashboards, alerts. No traces or logs.

Growing team (10 hosts + APM + basic logging)
Infrastructure Pro (10 hosts) $150/month
APM Pro (10 hosts) $310/month
Logs: 50GB/day ingestion ~$150/month
Logs: 10% indexed (150M events/mo) ~$255/month
Total monthly ~$865/month

Typical for a $1M ARR SaaS. Log costs grow fast — even with 10% indexing rate, logs are ~47% of the total bill.

Scale-up (25 hosts + full observability stack)
Infrastructure Enterprise (25 hosts) $575/month
APM Enterprise (25 hosts) $1,000/month
Logs (200GB/day ingestion + indexing) ~$2,000/month
Synthetics + RUM + other products ~$500/month
Total monthly ~$4,075/month

~$49,000/year. At this scale, most teams negotiate an annual enterprise contract for 20-40% discount.

How to Reduce Your Datadog Bill

Datadog costs are notoriously hard to predict. Here are the most effective cost control levers:

  • Use Flex Logs instead of Standard indexing — 97% cheaper for logs you rarely query
  • Archive to S3/GCS — pay only storage costs for compliance logs
  • Drop noisy logs at ingestion — use Logging without Limits to filter before indexing
  • Commit to annual contracts — typically 20–40% discount
  • Use tagging and exclusion filters — exclude health checks and debug logs from indexing
  • Monitor your own Datadog spend — set up budget alerts in Datadog's Usage page
