Descript Pricing 2026

Descript is an AI-powered video and podcast editing platform that revolutionizes editing by letting you edit as text. Instead of using traditional video timelines, you edit the transcript, and the video updates automatically. Perfect for creators, podcasters, and teams.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0/month (1 video/month, up to 3 hours, basic editing)
  • Creator: $24/month (unlimited videos, 150 GB storage, AI Overdub, filler word removal)
  • Pro: $48/month (Creator + team collab, screen recording, real-time editing, priority support)
  • Setup fee: $0 (no setup costs)
  • Best for: Content creators, podcasters, YouTubers, teams with video/podcast content

Descript Features by Plan

All Plans Include:

  • Text-based video editing (edit transcript, video updates)
  • Automatic transcription from video/audio
  • Multi-language support (100+ languages)
  • Basic captions and subtitles
  • Audio editing tools (trim, split, fade)

Creator Plan Adds:

  • Unlimited video/podcast editing
  • Descript Overdub (AI voice cloning)
  • Filler word removal (auto-remove "um", "like", "uh")
  • 150 GB cloud storage
  • Ripple editing (delete words, video auto-reflows)
  • Custom captions styling

Pro Plan Adds:

  • Real-time team collaboration
  • Screen recording built-in
  • Advanced speaker detection
  • Priority support
  • Advanced export options (ProRes, 4K)
  • Team workspace management

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Start with Free: Test Descript with 1 video/month before committing. Great for evaluating the text-based editing workflow.
  • Creator is the Sweet Spot: At $24/month, Creator offers unlimited editing + AI features. Best value for solo creators.
  • Pro Only for Teams: If you don't need real-time collaboration or screen recording, Creator is sufficient.
  • Compare to Adobe: Descript Creator ($24) vs Premiere ($22.49) is a tough choice. Descript wins on ease of use; Premiere wins on pro features.
  • Use CapCut Free if Budget-Constrained: CapCut is free and good for quick edits. But Descript's AI features (Overdub, filler removal) save time.
