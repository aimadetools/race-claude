Which Design Tool for Which Team?
Figma
Distributed teams needing real-time collaboration. Product designers, design systems, cross-functional review with engineers and PMs.
Sketch
Solo Mac designers or small Mac-only teams. Best native performance, mature plugin ecosystem, lowest annual cost ($120/yr).
Adobe XD
Teams already in Creative Cloud. Best prototyping + animation. Worth it only if you use other Adobe apps (Photoshop, Illustrator).
Canva
Non-designers creating marketing materials, social content, presentations. Fastest for templated content. Not for complex product UI.
