Drift Pricing 2026

Drift is a premium conversational marketing platform using AI chatbots and live chat to engage website visitors and qualify leads in real-time. Starting at $500/month for Starter and $1,000/month for Pro, Drift is positioned for B2B SaaS companies focused on revenue generation, not cost optimization.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free Trial: 30 days (full access, no credit card required)
  • Starter: $500/month (basic chatbots, live chat, email support)
  • Pro: $1,000/month (advanced AI, routing, integrations, priority support)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (unlimited everything, dedicated support)
  • Setup fee: $0 (no setup costs)
  • Best for: B2B SaaS companies, sales teams, revenue-focused businesses

Drift Features by Plan

  • AI-powered chatbots (no-code builder)
  • Live chat for real-time conversations
  • Visitor tracking and segmentation
  • Email integration (basic)
  • Mobile app access
  • Up to 2 team members

Pro Plan Adds:

  • Advanced AI and machine learning
  • Conversation routing to sales
  • Built-in CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
  • Calendar integration for demo scheduling

  • Up to 5 team members

Enterprise Adds:

  • Dedicated account manager

  • SLA (Service Level Agreement)
  • Custom training and onboarding
  • Unlimited team members

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Use 30-Day Free Trial: Test Drift thoroughly before committing. Evaluate if 2-3 qualified leads/month justify $500+.
  • Start with Starter: $500/month is minimum. Only upgrade to Pro if basic chatbots don't generate enough leads.
  • Track CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): Drift is an investment. Measure qualified leads and revenue impact monthly.
  • Combine with CRM: Drift integrates with Salesforce and HubSpot. Use integrations to avoid duplicate data entry.
  • Compare to Alternatives: LiveChat ($29/month) + HubSpot ($50/month) might be cheaper if you don't need AI-driven lead qualification.
  • Consider Team Size: Starter supports 2 members; Pro supports 5. Enterprise for unlimited. Choose based on actual team size.
