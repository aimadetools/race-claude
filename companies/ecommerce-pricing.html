E-Commerce Platform Pricing 2026

Shopify raised prices 33% in 2023 and added mandatory transaction fees. Etsy charges listing fees, transaction fees, and payment processing on every sale. WooCommerce looks free — until you add up hosting, plugins, and payment processing. Here's the real math.

⚠️ The hidden cost trap: transaction fees add up fast On $100,000/year in sales: Shopify Basic charges $2,900 in transaction fees (if not using Shopify Payments). Etsy charges ~$6,500 in combined fees. WooCommerce can be $0 in platform fees but $1,500+/yr in hosting + plugins. Know your numbers before you commit.

Which Platform for Which Seller
Shopify
Best for: Serious e-commerce businesses wanting full brand control, dropshipping, and scalability. Worth $39–$105/mo if you're selling consistently.
WooCommerce
Best for: WordPress users, developers, businesses that want zero platform fees and full customization. Budget $500–$2,000/yr for hosting + plugins.
Etsy
Best for: Handmade, vintage, or niche craft sellers who want access to Etsy's 95M buyers without building their own audience. High fees, but built-in traffic.
BigCommerce
Best for: High-volume stores that want 0% transaction fees and B2B/wholesale features. More complex than Shopify, better for scale.
