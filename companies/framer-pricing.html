Framer Pricing 2026

Framer is a modern web design and prototyping tool built for designers and teams. Start free with 3 projects, or upgrade to Pro ($20/mo per seat) for unlimited projects, custom domain hosting, and team collaboration.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: 3 projects, basic tools, framer.com subdomain
  • Pro: $20/mo per seat (annual billing: $12/mo)
  • Pro includes: Unlimited projects, custom domain, team collaboration, exports
  • Team pricing: $20/mo × number of team members
  • Best for: Designers, design agencies, rapid prototyping, responsive websites

Cost Tips for Teams

  • Use annual billing: 40% discount ($12/mo vs $20/mo) if billed yearly.
  • Start with Free: Test Framer with 3 projects before committing to Pro.
  • Add team members gradually: You only pay for active Pro seats, not a flat team fee.
  • Compare to Webflow: Framer Pro ($20/mo) is cheaper than Webflow ($30+/mo) and better for design teams.
  • Export to reduce tools: Framer Pro's export options reduce the need for separate design-to-code tools.
