FreshBooks Pricing 2026

FreshBooks is an invoicing and accounting platform built for freelancers, small businesses, and professional service firms. It combines invoicing, expense tracking, time tracking, and financial reporting in one platform. Four paid tiers scale from basic invoicing to advanced accounting with team collaboration and automation.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Lite: $15/month (invoicing + payment collection)
  • Plus: $30/month (accounting, time tracking, expense tracking) — Best value
  • Premium: $55/month (advanced features, automation, API access)
  • Select: Custom pricing (enterprise, unlimited users)
  • Free trial: 30 days free (no credit card required)
  • Best for: Freelancers, small businesses, service-based teams

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Start with Free Trial: FreshBooks offers 30 days free with full access. No credit card required. Use this to test all features before committing.
  • Lite Plan for Invoicing Only: If you only need to send invoices and collect payments, Lite at $15/mo is sufficient and half the cost of Plus.
  • Plus for Most Freelancers: Plus at $30/mo is the sweet spot for freelancers and small service businesses. It includes accounting and time tracking.
  • Integrate with Stripe/PayPal: FreshBooks payment processing saves time. When clients pay via invoice, funds go directly to your bank account.
  • Use Automated Billing: Premium plan includes recurring invoices. If you have recurring clients, Premium can save admin time.
