When to Upgrade Your Freshdesk Plan

  • At 1-2 agents: Free or Pro ($55/agent) makes sense. Free works for solopreneurs; Pro for small teams.
  • At 3-11 agents: Compare: Pro tier (3-11 × $55 = $165-605/mo) vs Growth's $180 minimum. Usually Pro is cheaper until you hit ~12 agents.
  • At 12+ agents: Growth tier ($15/agent) kicks in and becomes the best value.
  • At 50+ agents: Negotiate Enterprise contract. You'll likely pay $40-60/agent vs Growth's $15 (but with more features, SLAs, dedicated support).
