Freshsales Pricing 2026

Freshsales is a CRM platform built specifically for sales teams at SMBs. Unlike HubSpot (which bundles marketing + support), Freshsales focuses purely on sales with built-in phone calling, SMS, and AI-powered lead scoring. Pricing is per-user ($25–$65/month per person) with a 3-user minimum ($75–$195/month to start), making it competitive with Pipedrive while offering stronger built-in communication tools.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: Up to 3 users, basic CRM pipeline
  • Growth: $25/user/month (minimum 3 users = $75/mo), includes AI lead scoring
  • Pro: $65/user/month (minimum 3 users = $195/mo), includes advanced automation, unlimited lists
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (unlimited users, SSO, SLA)
  • Built-in: Phone calling, SMS, email tracking, AI lead scoring (on Growth+)

Freshsales' Competitive Advantages

  • Phone + SMS built-in: Unlike Pipedrive (charges $10–$30/mo extra), Freshsales includes calling and SMS on Growth+ tiers. No hidden charges.
  • AI lead scoring: Automatically prioritizes high-value prospects. No extra cost (included on Growth/Pro). Pipedrive requires advanced plan or third-party integration.
  • Affordable for SMB: $25/user/month entry point cheaper than HubSpot ($15/mo base but lacks phone/SMS). Better value than Zendesk ($49–$99/agent).
  • Pipeline automation: Custom deal stages, automation rules, multi-level approval. Pipedrive offers less automation without coding.
  • Native integrations: Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Zapier all included. No extra tools needed.
  • India-based support: 24/7 support with focus on SMB (vs HubSpot's sales-heavy model).

⚠️ Freshsales Pricing Considerations

  • 3-user minimum: Even if you only need 1 person, you pay for 3 ($75/mo). Add users sparingly to avoid surprise bills.
  • SMS + calling have overage limits: Growth plan includes 1,000 SMS/month, 500 minutes calling. Overage: $0.01/SMS, $0.50/calling minute. High-volume teams can run up surprise bills.
  • Per-user scaling gets expensive: At 20 people, Pro ($65 × 20) = $1,300/mo. Pipedrive ($99 × 20) = $1,980, but HubSpot might be comparable at that scale.
  • No customization without code: Unlike no-code CRM builders (Airtable), you can't build custom workflows without development help.
  • Free tier is crippled: Only 3 users max, no phone/SMS, no AI scoring. Not useful for long-term; forces upgrade.
