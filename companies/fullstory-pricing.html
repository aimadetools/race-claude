FullStory Pricing 2026

FullStory is a premium digital experience intelligence platform that captures complete user sessions with AI-powered analytics. Targeted at mid-market and enterprise teams, FullStory provides advanced session replay, error tracking, and predictive insights starting at $50/month, with most teams paying $300-$999/month for production-level coverage.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Starter: $50/mo (1K sessions/month) — No free tier
  • Pro (Growth): $300–$999/mo (5K–50K+ sessions/month)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (unlimited, SSO, SLA)
  • All tiers include: Session replay, error capture, heatmaps, basic AI insights
  • Key differentiator: AI anomaly detection, rage click scoring, advanced error intelligence

FullStory's Competitive Advantages

  • AI-powered anomaly detection: Automatically flags unusual user behavior, errors, and rage clicks without manual setup. Competitors (Heap, Hotjar) lack this.
  • Advanced error intelligence: Native integration with error stacks, breadcrumbs, and reproduction steps. Better than LogRocket for enterprise debugging.
  • Enterprise security: HIPAA, SOC2 Type II, FedRAMP available. Required for healthcare, finance, and regulated industries. Heap/Hotjar lack these certifications.
  • Session reconstruction: Captures full user journey across multiple pages/devices. Detects where users get stuck.
  • Premium support: Dedicated account managers on Pro+ plans. 24/7 phone support for Enterprise. Heap offers async email only.
  • Pricing transparency: No surprise overages — sessions are metered upfront. Hotjar charges surprise data overage fees; FullStory does not.

⚠️ FullStory Cost Considerations

  • No free tier: Unlike Heap or Hotjar, FullStory doesn't offer a free plan. $50/mo minimum to get started.
  • High-traffic sites get expensive fast: At 100K+ sessions/month, you could pay $3K–$5K+/mo. Evaluate Heap ($249/mo unlimited) for comparison.
  • Minimum commitment: Many enterprise teams are locked into annual contracts at discounted rates. Negotiate if signing long-term.
  • Data retention costs: Storing 90+ days of session data on Pro+ can add 10–20% to your bill. Heap offers unlimited retention at flat rate.
