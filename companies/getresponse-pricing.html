GetResponse Pricing 2026

GetResponse is an all-in-one email marketing, automation, landing pages, and webinar platform. Start free with 500 contacts, scale to enterprise with unlimited features on all tiers. Better value than Mailchimp for small businesses.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: 500 contacts, 1K emails/month, landing pages, basic automation
  • Basic: $15/mo (1K contacts)
  • Plus: $48/mo (5K contacts)
  • Professional: $98/mo (25K contacts)
  • Max: $299/mo (100K+ contacts)
  • All plans include: Email, automation, landing pages, webinars
  • Annual discount: 18% off (pay yearly)

Why Choose GetResponse?

  • All-in-one platform: Email + landing pages + webinars + automation. No need for separate tools (unlike Mailchimp).
  • Free tier is generous: 500 contacts forever free with real features (not a crippled trial).
  • Better than Mailchimp at scale: GetResponse includes landing pages and webinars on all plans. Mailchimp charges extra.
  • Webinars included: No need to buy Zoom or GoToWebinar separately. Hosted webinars built-in.
  • 18% annual discount: Pay yearly for better rates ($15.50 → $12.30/mo for annual).
