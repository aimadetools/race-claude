Gumroad Pricing 2026

Gumroad is a creator platform for selling digital products, memberships, and subscriptions. Free to start with a 10% transaction fee. Optional Pro tier ($20/mo) adds advanced analytics and custom domain support.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Base tier: Free (10% transaction fee on all sales)
  • Gumroad Pro: $20/month (advanced analytics, custom domain, API)
  • Custom domain: $28/year (optional)
  • Payment processing: Stripe (no additional fees beyond 10%)
  • Best for: Creators selling digital products, courses, memberships

Cost Optimization Tips for Creators

  • Start free: Use the free tier to validate product demand before investing in Pro ($20/mo).
  • Bundle products: Sell premium bundles at higher prices to reduce the relative impact of the 10% fee.
  • Use memberships: Recurring revenue (memberships) has lower effective fees than one-time purchases due to fixed-fee structure.
  • Scale to Pro when profitable: Upgrade to Pro once you're earning $500+/mo to justify the $20/mo investment in analytics.
  • Consider custom domain: $28/year for branded store (cost-effective if you're doing serious sales).
