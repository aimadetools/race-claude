Heap's Unique Advantages

  • No code required: Automatic event capture. Define events retroactively without engineer involvement. Major advantage over Mixpanel/Amplitude.
  • Session replay quality: High-quality video playbacks with heatmaps overlayed. Better UX than Fullstory for finding issues.
  • Retroactive analysis: Create new funnels and cohorts from existing data without waiting for new data collection.
  • Better than Hotjar for analytics: Hotjar is focused on feedback; Heap is focused on behavioral analytics (opposite goals).
  • Simpler than Google Analytics 4: 10x easier to understand and get insights from user sessions.
