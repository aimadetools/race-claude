Which Should You Choose?

Choose HubSpot if: You need an integrated CRM + support solution. You have a small-to-medium support team (under 10 agents). Cost is a concern. You want a flat-rate model.

Choose Zendesk if: You need a dedicated support platform. You have multi-channel support needs (email, chat, phone, social). You're a large enterprise with many agents. You want the best-in-class support solution.

Key insight: HubSpot wins on price for small teams due to its flat-rate model. Zendesk is more feature-rich for support but charges per agent, making it expensive at scale. HubSpot's integrated CRM adds value HubSpot is a no-brainer for cost-conscious teams.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is HubSpot or Zendesk cheaper?

For small-to-medium support teams, HubSpot is much cheaper due to its flat-rate model. A 5-agent team pays $1,440/year for HubSpot vs $5,940/year for Zendesk. However, Zendesk is more specialized for large support organizations where its per-agent pricing may offer better features.

What's Zendesk's pricing increase from November 2025?

Zendesk raised prices in November 2025. Team plan went from $49→$55/agent (+12%), Professional from $89→$99/agent (+11%). These are meaningful increases for teams already on paid plans.

Does HubSpot include support ticket management?

Yes, but it's basic. HubSpot's Service Hub includes ticketing, but Zendesk is more robust for dedicated support teams. HubSpot shines when you need CRM + support integrated.

Can I use HubSpot's free plan for support?

Yes, HubSpot's free tier includes basic support features (ticketing, live chat). It's good for testing but limited. Zendesk doesn't offer a free plan, only a 14-day trial.

What if my support team grows from 5 to 10 agents?

HubSpot: Cost stays at $120/month. You can add unlimited agents at no extra cost.
Zendesk: Cost doubles from $495/month to $990/month. Each additional agent costs $99/month.
