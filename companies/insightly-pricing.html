Insightly Pricing 2026

Insightly is a cloud-based CRM and project management platform designed for small and mid-size teams. It combines customer relationship management, project tracking, and workflow automation in one platform. A free Lite tier is available, with paid plans starting at $29/user/month.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Lite (Free): $0/month (up to 2 users)
  • Plus: $29/user/month (minimum 3 users = $87/month)
  • Professional: $49/user/month (minimum 3 users = $147/month)
  • Business: $99/user/month (advanced security, analytics)
  • Discount: 15% off if paid annually
  • Best for: Small teams needing CRM + project management combined

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Start with Lite: The free tier supports up to 2 users and includes CRM + project management. Perfect for testing the platform before committing to paid plans.
  • Pay Annually for Savings: Get 15% off if you commit to annual billing. For Plus at $29/user/month, annual billing costs $295/user/year.
  • Limit Users to Essentials: Only add users who need full CRM/project access. Consider shared logins for view-only stakeholders.
  • Compare to HubSpot: For teams of 3+, Insightly Plus ($87/month) beats HubSpot Starter ($50/month for 1 user) when scaling. For 1-2 users, consider HubSpot Free.
  • Use Workflows to Reduce Manual Work: Professional tier includes advanced automation that saves time on data entry and follow-ups.
