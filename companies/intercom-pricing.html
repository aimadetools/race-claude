Frequently Asked Questions

Does Intercom have a free plan in 2026?

No. Intercom discontinued their free plan for new customers in 2023. They offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. The minimum paid plan (Essential) is $47/seat/month billed annually as of January 2026.

How does Intercom compare to Zendesk pricing?

Zendesk Suite Team starts at $55/agent/month (annual) — comparable to Intercom Essential. Zendesk Suite Growth is $89/agent. For comparable feature sets, the tools are similarly priced, though Zendesk's ticketing is considered stronger while Intercom's in-app messaging and AI features are more mature.

Is Intercom pricing worth it for small teams?

At $47/seat/month, Intercom Essential is expensive for early-stage startups. Alternatives like Crisp ($25/month flat for teams), Chatwoot (open-source self-hosted), and Help Scout ($22/user/month) are commonly compared. Intercom's advantage is brand recognition with customers and a mature feature set.

How does Intercom's AI pricing work?

Since Q3 2025, Intercom charges for "AI resolutions" — conversations where Fin AI (their bot) resolves a support inquiry without human intervention. Each plan includes a number of AI resolutions; additional resolutions cost extra per unit. High-volume teams may find AI resolution costs add meaningfully to their monthly bill.
