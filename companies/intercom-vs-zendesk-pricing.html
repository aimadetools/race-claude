Which Should You Choose?

Choose Intercom if: You need in-app messaging and live chat as core features. Your team uses AI heavily for support automation (Fin AI is stronger than Zendesk AI). You're okay with per-seat costs that scale with headcount.

Choose Zendesk if: You focus on ticket-based support workflows. You need a community forum or knowledge base integration. You want more flexibility in routing channels (email, chat, voice, SMS). Enterprise support is critical.

The real cost difference: For a 5-person team, Intercom Essential ($2,820/year) is cheaper than Zendesk Team ($3,300/year) — but this flips at mid-tier. Intercom Advanced ($7,140/year) costs significantly more than Zendesk Professional ($5,940/year). Both raised prices recently, so compare actual contract costs before switching.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Intercom or Zendesk cheaper?

For entry-level teams, Intercom Essential ($2,820/year for 5 seats) is slightly cheaper than Zendesk Team ($3,300/year). But for mid-market teams, Zendesk Professional ($5,940) is cheaper than Intercom Advanced ($7,140). Compare your specific team size and feature needs before deciding.

Why did Intercom raise prices 20% in January 2026?

Intercom cited investment in AI features (Fin AI, summarization, suggested replies) as the reason. The increase affected Essential, Advanced, and Expert tiers equally. Existing customers received 30 days notice but no grandfathering.

Does Zendesk have the same AI features as Intercom?

Zendesk AI (formerly Einstein) is newer than Fin AI and focuses on suggested replies and ticket categorization. Intercom's Fin AI is more mature for proactive conversations and resolution automation. If AI is critical, Intercom currently has an edge, but the gap is closing.

Can I use Zendesk for in-app messaging like Intercom?

Zendesk Web Widget provides basic chat, but for true in-app messaging (embedded surveys, contextual prompts, app-native messaging), you need Intercom or a dedicated tool like Appcues. Zendesk's strength is ticketing, not in-app engagement.

What happens if I outgrow Zendesk Team?

You upgrade to Professional ($99/agent) or Enterprise (custom). Unlike Intercom, Zendesk doesn't dramatically increase per-agent cost between tiers, so the upgrade path is more predictable.
