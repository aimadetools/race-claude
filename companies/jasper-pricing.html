Jasper pricing uses a credit system: Each plan includes monthly credits, and credits regenerate monthly. Overages cost extra. Starter is best for small teams; Professional for agencies.

Understanding Jasper Credits

What are credits? Each word generated in Jasper costs 1 credit. A typical 100-word blog post uses ~100 credits. Credits don't roll over—they reset monthly.

  • Starter (25K credits): ~250–300 blog posts/month (100 words each), or unlimited short-form content
  • Professional (100K credits): ~1,000–1,200 blog posts, or 50+ long-form assets + campaigns
  • Business (250K+ credits): Unlimited long-form content + campaigns + API usage

Overage cost: Extra credits beyond your plan cost ~$0.01 per credit. If you go over 25K credits on Starter, each additional credit costs ~$0.01.

Jasper vs Alternatives

Jasper ($39/mo Starter) is one of the most affordable AI copywriting tools. Excels at long-form content (blog posts, email campaigns). Best for content teams and agencies.

Copy.ai ($49/mo) has similar pricing but lower credit limits. Great for short-form copy (ads, social media). Cheaper overage system.

ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo) is cheaper but requires more manual prompting. Best if you're skilled at prompt engineering. No team collaboration features.

Notion AI ($10/user/mo) is much cheaper but limited to document editing within Notion. Not designed for standalone content generation.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Jasper credit? How many words = 1 credit?

1 credit = 1 word generated by Jasper. A 100-word blog paragraph costs 100 credits. A 1,000-word article costs 1,000 credits. Starter's 25K credits = roughly 250 articles or 500 shorter blog posts.

Can I carry over unused credits to next month?

No. Jasper credits reset monthly. Unused credits are lost. If you consistently go over, upgrade to the next tier. If you consistently stay under, downgrade to save money.

Is Jasper Professional ($99/mo) worth it over Starter ($39/mo)?

Professional is worth it if: (1) you generate 1,000+ words/month regularly, (2) you need API access, (3) you run multiple campaigns. Otherwise, Starter + occasional overages is cheaper. Overages on Starter are ~$0.01 per credit, so 25K extra credits = $250, making Professional a bargain for high-volume users.

Does Jasper offer a free trial or free tier?

Jasper offers a 7-day free trial with access to all features at full credit limits. No permanent free tier. After the trial, you must choose a paid plan.

Can multiple people use one Jasper account?

Yes. Starter and Professional plans support team members. Business plan allows unlimited team members. Each team member logs in with their own account and shares the credit pool.
