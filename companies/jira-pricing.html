Frequently Asked Questions

Is Jira free?

Jira Cloud has a free tier for teams up to 10 users. It includes issue tracking, basic workflows, and 2GB storage. At 11+ users, you must upgrade to Standard ($7.50/user/mo). Enterprise deployments require custom quotes.

How much does Jira cost for enterprise?

Enterprise pricing is custom-quoted based on team size, deployment model (Cloud vs Server vs Data Center), and feature needs. Large enterprises typically negotiate $4–8/user/month or flat annual licenses. Contact Atlassian sales for quotes.

Can I use Jira Server instead of Cloud?

No — Atlassian discontinued Server licenses in 2024. Jira Server customers must migrate to Cloud (SaaS) or Data Center (self-hosted). Cloud is per-user/month; Data Center is per-instance/year (typically $25K+).

What's the difference between Jira Standard and Premium?

Premium ($12.50/user) adds portfolio management, advanced permissions, custom fields, and priority support. Standard is sufficient for most teams; upgrade to Premium if you need cross-project visibility or complex governance.

Does Jira offer discounts?

Yes — 30% discount on annual billing (already reflected above). No other standard discounts; volume discounts only apply to Enterprise contracts ($100K+/year).
