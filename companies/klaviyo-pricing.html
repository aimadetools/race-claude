Klaviyo Pricing 2026

Klaviyo is an email and SMS marketing platform designed specifically for e-commerce. Pricing scales with contact list size, with SMS and e-commerce integrations included on all plans.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0 (up to 500 contacts, unlimited emails and SMS)
  • Standard: $20/month (1,000-5,000 contacts)
  • Plus: $65/month (5,000-10,000 contacts)
  • Pro: $130/month (10,000-25,000 contacts)
  • SMS per message: $0.01–0.02 (billed separately)
  • Best for: Shopify stores, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and e-commerce automation

Why Klaviyo is Best for E-Commerce

  • Native E-Commerce Integrations: Deep Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento integrations. Syncs product catalogs, abandoned carts, orders automatically.
  • SMS Included: No extra cost for SMS like Mailchimp ($20+/month extra). SMS + email campaigns in one platform.
  • Abandoned Cart Automation: Triggers automatic SMS/email when customers leave items in cart. Recovers 10–15% of lost revenue.
  • Post-Purchase Flows: Order confirmation, shipping notifications, review requests automatically sent.
  • Predictive Analytics: Identifies high-value customers, churn risk, lifetime value predictions.
  • Lower Cost at Scale: $130/month for 20K contacts beats Mailchimp at $400+/month.
