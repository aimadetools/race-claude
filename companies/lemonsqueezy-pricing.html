Lemon Squeezy Pricing 2026

Lemon Squeezy is an all-in-one platform designed for indie creators, course authors, and SaaS founders who want to sell digital products, subscriptions, and memberships without friction. There's no monthly fee—you only pay a small transaction fee (8% + $0.50) when you make a sale. This makes it ideal for bootstrapped creators and founders testing new products with zero upfront investment.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Monthly Cost: $0 (completely free to use)
  • Per-Sale Fee: 8% of transaction + $0.50 (only charged when you sell)
  • No hidden fees: No setup, no minimums, no monthly charges
  • All features included: Digital products, subscriptions, memberships, affiliate system, email delivery, API access
  • White-label option: Available for high-volume sellers (custom pricing)

Why Lemon Squeezy Wins for Indie Creators

  • Zero friction to start: No monthly fees, no credit card to register, instant payout to bank account. Test product ideas risk-free.
  • Creator-first features: Affiliate system built-in (recruit partners to sell for you), license key generation (for software), email delivery (send digital files instantly).

  • API + Webhooks: Integrate with your own site, Zapier, Discord, Slack. Not locked into Lemon Squeezy UI.
