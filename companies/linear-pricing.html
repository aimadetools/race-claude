Frequently Asked Questions

Does Linear have a free plan in 2026?

Yes, but it's significantly more limited than before. After February 2026, the free plan is capped at 10 members and no longer includes GitHub or Slack integrations. Larger teams or those needing integrations must upgrade to Business ($10/member/month annual).

What is Linear's new Scale tier?

Scale ($16/member/month annual) sits between Business and Enterprise. It includes advanced workflow automation, priority support, SLA guarantees, and higher API rate limits. It's aimed at fast-growing engineering teams that don't yet need full Enterprise compliance features.

How does Linear pricing compare to Jira?

Jira Standard is $7.75/user/month (annual) for up to 35,000 users — significantly cheaper than Linear Business at $10/member. However, Linear's UX is widely considered superior for product-led teams. Many startups choose Linear for speed and interface quality despite the higher cost.

Does Linear offer monthly billing?

Yes. Monthly billing is available at a higher per-seat rate (typically 15-20% more than annual). Prices shown above are for annual billing.
