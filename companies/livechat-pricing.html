LiveChat Pricing 2026

LiveChat is a live chat software platform for real-time customer support. With a free plan (ad-supported), Starter at $29/month for up to 3 agents, and Business at $59/month for up to 10 agents, LiveChat is an affordable alternative to expensive customer support platforms. Includes AI chatbots, ticketing, and customer analytics.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0/month (1 agent, ad-supported, 300 visitors/month limit)
  • Starter: $29/month (up to 3 agents, unlimited visitors, basic analytics)
  • Business: $59/month (up to 10 agents, advanced features, AI chatbots, automation)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (50+ agents, dedicated support, SSO)
  • Setup fee: $0 (no setup costs)
  • Best for: Small to mid-size customer support teams, startups, e-commerce

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Free Plan is Real: LiveChat's free plan is fully functional. If you're testing or have light traffic, start free.
  • Starter at $29 is a Great Value: Unlimited visitors, 3 agents, basic features. Much cheaper than Zendesk ($147+).
  • AI Chatbots in Business Plan: At $59/month, Business includes AI chatbots. These can reduce support volume by 20-30%.
  • Compare to Crisp: LiveChat Starter ($29) vs Crisp Starter (€25). LiveChat has more features; Crisp has unlimited agents.
  • Annual Billing Discount: Pay annually to save 10-15% vs monthly billing.
  • Use Integrations: LiveChat integrates with Slack, HubSpot, Zapier. Connect your existing tools.
