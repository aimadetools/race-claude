The email marketing showdown: Mailchimp for SMBs and free-tier loyalty, Brevo for advanced automation and SMS, and ConvertKit for creators and newsletter writers.

  • Mailchimp Free: $0 (500 subscribers max, free tier cut in Feb 2024 but still available)
  • Mailchimp Premium: Starts at $20/month (5,000 subscribers)

  • Best for: Mailchimp = SMBs starting free; Brevo = email + SMS automation; ConvertKit = creators and newsletter writers

When to Choose Each Platform

Choose Mailchimp If:

  • You want to start completely free
  • SMB with under 5,000 contacts
  • Need many integrations (1,500+)
  • Prefer intuitive, simple UI
  • Don't need SMS or advanced automation
  • Want reliable established platform

Choose Brevo If:

  • You need SMS + email together
  • Large lists (50,000+) on budget
  • Advanced automation essential
  • Want best value at scale
  • Need transactional emails
  • Willing to invest in learning curve

Choose ConvertKit If:

  • You're a creator or newsletter writer
  • Want creator-friendly features
  • Audience building is priority
  • Value simplicity over features
  • Don't need SMS or SMS marketing
  • Under 10,000 subscribers sweet spot

💰 Cost Verdict

Cheapest for startups (free tier): ConvertKit free plan supports up to 1,000 subscribers at $0

Best value under 5,000 subscribers: Mailchimp $20/mo essentials plan

Best value at scale (50,000+ subs): Brevo at $48/mo (includes SMS, transactional email, advanced automation)

Best for creators: ConvertKit at $25/mo for full automation + audience building

Pro tip: Start free, then move to Mailchimp or ConvertKit. Only switch to Brevo if you need SMS + email bundling or have massive lists. Mailchimp's free tier cuts in 2024 makes ConvertKit's free tier (1,000 subs) more generous for creators.

Frequently Asked Questions

Did Mailchimp really cut its free tier in 2024?

Yes. Mailchimp removed free plan automations, landing pages, and ads in Feb 2024. Free tier is now basic email campaigns only. Premium required for automation. This pushed many creators to ConvertKit.

Which has the best free tier in 2026?

ConvertKit (1,000 subscribers, full email + automation) > Brevo (300 contacts, unlimited emails) > Mailchimp (500 contacts, basic campaigns only).

Can I use all three together?

Yes. Many use Mailchimp for transactional/product emails, ConvertKit for newsletters, and Brevo for SMS campaigns. Integration via Zapier or webhooks is possible but adds cost.

Which has the best deliverability?

Brevo is industry-leading for deliverability (formerly Sendinblue). ConvertKit is very good for creators. Mailchimp is good but has reputation issues at scale. Test each with your email list.

Can I import lists from one platform to another?

Yes. All three support CSV imports and exports. Plan a test send to your domain before fully importing to avoid spam folder issues.
