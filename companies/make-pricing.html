Operations & Overage Pricing

What Counts as an Operation?

  • One workflow execution = 1 operation (regardless of complexity)
  • Each action within the workflow counts as 1 operation
  • Example: Trigger + 5 actions = 6 operations per execution

Overage Costs

Extra operations (beyond plan) $0.10 per 1,000 operations
Storage overage (beyond 100 MB) $0.50 per GB/month

Make vs Zapier Pricing Comparison

At 50,000 operations/month:

  • Make Pro: $18.48/mo (exact fit for 50K ops)
  • Zapier Pro: $99/mo (300 tasks × 100 ops each = 30K ops, needs Business at $299/mo for 750 tasks)
  • Savings: 81% cheaper per operation

Make's pricing is based on operations/month; Zapier's is based on tasks (workflows). At scale, Make is significantly cheaper. However, Zapier has more advanced features (scenarios vs workflows) and better app integrations.

When to Upgrade Tiers

Choose Make tier based on estimated monthly operations:

  • Free (1,000 ops): Personal projects, experimenting
  • Standard (10,000 ops): Small business, fewer than 10 active automations
  • Pro (50,000 ops): Growing team, 10-50 automations (most common)
  • Business (250,000 ops): Mid-market, 50-200 automations or complex workflows
  • Enterprise: Large organization with 200+ automations and need for custom SLAs
