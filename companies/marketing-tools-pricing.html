Marketing Tool Pricing 2026

Typeform raised prices 67%. Mailchimp's free plan got gutted (2,000 → 500 contacts). Grammarly increased Business pricing. SEO tools like Ahrefs and Semrush are $100–$500/mo and rising. Here's what the full marketing stack actually costs today.

⚠️ Email marketing pricing is contact-based — it scales fast Most email tools charge by contacts or sends. A list of 10,000 contacts costs $50–$100/mo on Mailchimp, $25/mo on Brevo. As your list grows, costs jump dramatically. Bait-and-switch free plans are common — know the pricing tiers before you build your list on a platform.

Which Tool for Which Marketer
Mailchimp
Best for: Small businesses just starting with email. Familiar UI, many integrations. Switch to Brevo once your list exceeds 2,500 contacts.
Brevo
Best for: Growing lists — email-based pricing means you're not penalized for list size. Best value for high-send-volume, mid-size lists.
Typeform
Best for: Brand-forward lead gen forms, conversational surveys. Premium experience — but Google Forms is free if you just need basic forms.
Grammarly
Best for: Marketing teams producing lots of written content. Premium worth it for content marketers; Business worth it for 3+ writers on a team.
Ahrefs
Best for: SEO-first teams, link building, keyword research. Slightly better backlink data than Semrush.
Semrush
Best for: Agencies, content teams, PPC + SEO combined. More marketing features beyond pure SEO. Pricier but broader toolset.
