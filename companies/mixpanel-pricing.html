Mixpanel Pricing 2026

Mixpanel is a product analytics platform offering event tracking, user segmentation, funnel analysis, and retention insights. Pricing is based on monthly tracked users (MTU) with a generous free tier for early-stage products.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0 (unlimited events, 500 MTU, 30-day retention)
  • Growth: $999/month (up to 250K MTU, all core analytics features)
  • Pro: $2,999/month (1M+ MTU, advanced features, priority support)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (unlimited MTU, custom features, dedicated support)
  • MTU definition: Unique users tracked in a given month
  • Best for: SaaS products, mobile apps, and websites needing user behavior analytics

Why Use Mixpanel?

  • Generous free tier: Unlimited events up to 500 MTU. Test for free without credit card.
  • User-friendly UX: Easiest product analytics tool to learn and use. Funnels and cohorts require no SQL.
  • Retention analysis: Built-in retention curves and cohort tracking. Understand which users stay vs churn.
  • A/B testing: Native experiment tracking for conversion optimization.
  • Affordable for mid-scale: Growth tier ($999/mo) supports up to 250K MTU. Amplitude would cost $1,995+ at the same scale.
  • Mobile app analytics: First-class mobile SDKs. Tracks iOS and Android native events seamlessly.
