What You Get in Each Plan

Free Plan

  • Moz Local (limited): 1 location listing
  • Keyword Explorer: 10 free searches/month
  • MozBar: Basic SEO toolbar for Chrome/Firefox
  • Community reports: Limited access
  • No rank tracking, no competitor analysis

Standard Plan ($99/mo or $74.25/mo annual)

  • ✅ Keyword Explorer: Unlimited searches, keyword difficulty scores, SERP analysis
  • ✅ Site Crawl: Monthly site audits for on-page optimization opportunities
  • ✅ Rank Tracking: Track up to 300 keywords across multiple locations
  • ✅ Backlink Analysis: Link research, competitor backlink analysis (limited)
  • ✅ Moz Local: Manage up to 5 locations, review monitoring
  • ✅ API Access: Integrate Moz data into your workflows
  • ✅ MozBar Premium: Enhanced SEO toolbar with metrics
  • ❌ White-label reports
  • ❌ Advanced competitor analysis

Professional Plan ($179/mo or ~$134.25/mo annual)

  • ✅ Everything in Standard, plus:
  • ✅ Rank Tracking: Track up to 1,000 keywords (vs 300)
  • ✅ Moz Local: Manage up to 25 locations (vs 5)
  • ✅ Backlink Analysis: Full Moz Link Index access (500M+ links), detailed competitor link reports
  • ✅ Advanced Reporting: Custom reports, PDF exports
  • ✅ White-label: Client-facing reports with your branding
  • ✅ Priority Support: Faster response times

Enterprise Plan (Custom)

  • ✅ Everything in Professional
  • ✅ Custom implementation and onboarding
  • ✅ Dedicated account manager
  • ✅ Training and consulting
  • ✅ Custom keyword tracking (unlimited)
  • ✅ SLA guarantees

Key Moz Features Explained

Keyword Explorer

Moz's core tool. Unlimited monthly searches to evaluate keyword difficulty, search volume, click-through rate (CTR) potential, and opportunity. Perfect for finding low-competition keywords to target.

Site Crawl

Technical SEO audit of your website. Identifies on-page optimization issues, broken links, crawl errors, and missing title/meta descriptions. Standard plan includes monthly crawls; Professional includes weekly.

Rank Tracking

Monitor where your keywords rank in Google. Standard tracks 300 keywords; Professional tracks 1,000. Includes local rank tracking and alerts when rankings change significantly.

Backlink Analysis

Analyze your backlink profile and competitors' links. Moz indexes 500M+ links. Standard plan has limited competitor link data; Professional has full access. Critical for link building strategy.

Moz Local

For multi-location businesses. Manage local listings, monitor reviews across platforms, and track local search rankings. Standard: 5 locations; Professional: 25 locations.

MozBar

Chrome/Firefox extension that shows SEO metrics while browsing. Premium version shows domain authority, page authority, spam score, and backlink data on any page.

When to Choose Moz

✅ Choose Moz if:

  • • You're a small to mid-size SEO team ($891/year is 25% cheaper than Ahrefs)
  • • Your primary need is keyword research + rank tracking
  • • You want 25% annual discount (best among the three)
  • • You're not doing deep competitor backlink analysis
  • • You manage multi-location businesses (Moz Local is best-in-class)
  • • You need white-label reports for clients (Professional plan)

⚠️ Choose Ahrefs if you need:

  • • Best-in-class backlink analysis (192B+ indexed links vs Moz's 500M)
  • • Content gap analysis for competitors
  • • Budget willing to spend $129+/mo

📊 Choose Semrush if you need:

  • • All-in-one platform (SEO + PPC + social media + content marketing)
  • • PPC/Ad research alongside SEO
  • • Most comprehensive feature set at a higher price
