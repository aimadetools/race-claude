New Relic Pricing 2026

New Relic is a full-stack observability platform offering application performance monitoring (APM), infrastructure monitoring, logs, and synthetic monitoring. Pricing is based on data ingestion volume with flexible plans.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0 (100 GB/month, 7-day retention, basic features)
  • Standard: $99/month (includes 100 GB ingestion, all products: APM, infrastructure, logs, synthetics)
  • Pro: $299/month (90-day retention, advanced features, higher API limits)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (unlimited, dedicated support, custom features)
  • Overage cost: ~$0.50–1.50/GB over included limit
  • Best for: Development and DevOps teams needing full-stack visibility

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Use sampling: Don't ingest 100% of transactions. Sample 10% in production, 100% in dev.
  • Archive logs: Send old logs to S3 after 30 days to stay within ingestion budget.
  • Filter data: Exclude verbose DEBUG logs. Only ingest WARN+ in production.
  • Monitor ingestion: Set budgets in New Relic UI. Get daily alerts when approaching limits.
  • Use free tier: Perfect for development. Upgrade to Standard only when in production with 50+ GB/month.
