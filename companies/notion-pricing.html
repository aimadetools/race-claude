Frequently Asked Questions

Does Notion offer a free plan?

Yes. Notion's free plan includes unlimited individual use, but limits collaboration to 10 guests and restricts certain advanced features. It's suitable for personal use but teams typically need Plus or Business.

How often does Notion change its pricing?

Based on our monitoring data, Notion has made significant pricing changes approximately once per year. However, smaller changes — like feature gating, guest limits, and grandfathering rules — happen more frequently and with less notice.

Does Notion notify users before a price increase?

No — the March 2026 increase was not announced by email in advance. Users discovered it when visiting the pricing page. Notion's terms allow price changes with 30 days notice for new billing cycles, but the specific timing of notifications varies.

What's the difference between Notion Plus and Business?

Plus ($10/mo annual) includes unlimited blocks, file uploads, and 30-day page history. Business ($18/mo annual) adds SAML SSO, advanced analytics, private team spaces, and longer page history. Enterprise includes audit logs, custom data retention, and dedicated support.

Is Notion pricing per user or per workspace?

Per member for paid plans. Each team member who needs full edit access requires a paid seat. Guests (view-only) are free up to the plan's guest limit.
