QuickBooks Pricing 2026

QuickBooks Online is the leading cloud-based accounting software for small businesses, self-employed professionals, and freelancers. It provides invoicing, expense tracking, financial reporting, tax preparation, and optional payroll integration. Three paid tiers scale from basic invoicing to multi-user accounting with unlimited users.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Simple Start: $30/month (invoicing, expense tracking, basic reports)
  • Plus: $80/month (advanced reports, up to 3 users, budget tracking)
  • Advanced: $180/month (unlimited users, custom roles, API access)
  • Discount: 50% off first 3 months (new customers)
  • Payroll add-on: $45–$315/month depending on employee count
  • Best for: Small business accounting, freelancers, service-based businesses

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Take the 50% New Customer Discount: New QuickBooks customers get 50% off the first 3 months. That's $15/mo for Simple Start, $40/mo for Plus, or $90/mo for Advanced.
  • Start with Simple Start: If you're just invoicing and tracking expenses, Simple Start at $30/mo is sufficient. Upgrade to Plus only when you need multi-user access or advanced reporting.
  • Use Built-in Tax Prep Tools: All QuickBooks plans include tax report preparation. This saves accountant fees ($1,000+/year).
  • Integrate with Your Bank: QuickBooks auto-reconciles with your bank account, saving hours on manual bookkeeping.
  • Avoid QuickBooks Desktop: QuickBooks Desktop is being phased out. QuickBooks Online is cheaper and more feature-rich ($30/mo vs. $180+ one-time purchase).
