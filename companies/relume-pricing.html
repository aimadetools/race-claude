Relume Pricing 2026

Relume is an AI-powered web design tool built for Webflow users who want to design faster. With 100+ AI-generated component templates, auto-generated copy, and Webflow integration, Relume cuts design time from weeks to days. The tool is free forever for individuals, with optional paid plans for teams and agencies who need collaboration and advanced AI features.

Why Designers Choose Relume

  • AI-first design: Generate entire components from text prompts. "Create a testimonial carousel" → done in seconds. Beats manual Figma work by 10x.
  • Webflow integration: Clone designs directly into Webflow. No hand-off, no developer needed. Design stays responsive.
  • 100+ pre-designed templates: Use as starting points. Modify copy, colors, layout. Free to use, no AI credits needed.
  • AI copywriting: Generate landing page copy, button text, headers. Save writer/copywriter time.
  • Affordable team collaboration: Teams plan ($20) vs hiring second designer ($4K+/mo). Massive leverage.
  • No learning curve: If you know Webflow, you know Relume. Minimal training needed.

How Relume Compares

  • vs Framer: Framer is a full prototyping tool (free + $15/mo). Relume is Webflow-specific. Choose Framer for interactive prototypes, Relume for Webflow-native design.
  • vs Figma plugins: Figma has AI plugins (Diagram, etc.) but charge per-feature. Relume is cheaper ($20/mo all-in) and purpose-built for Webflow.
  • vs hiring a designer: Junior designer = $3K–$5K/mo. Relume Teams ($20/mo) + your time = 1% the cost. Forces you to learn design faster.
  • vs Webflow AI (coming soon): Webflow is building AI features natively. Relume will likely stay independent; worth $20/mo if they innovate faster.
