Render's Key Advantages

  • Free tier doesn't disappear: Auto-pauses but persists (unlike Heroku which killed free tier entirely)
  • Database included: Free PostgreSQL with paid web service tiers (no separate $9+ database charge)
  • Transparent pricing: Pay only for what you use, no surprise Dyno charges
  • Better infrastructure: Built on modern cloud (vs. Heroku's older platform)
  • Deploy via GitHub: Auto-deploy on git push, just like Heroku
  • Environment variables: Easy secrets management built in

Render's Limitations

  • Smaller ecosystem: Fewer add-ons and integrations than Heroku (but growing)
  • Less mature: Heroku has 13+ years of edge case handling; Render is newer
  • Support: Heroku has more community resources and Stack Overflow answers
  • Free tier pauses: App goes to sleep after 15 minutes inactivity (fine for side projects, not prod)

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my free tier app pause on Render?

Yes. Free tier services pause after 15 minutes of inactivity. Paid tiers ($7+) run 24/7 without pausing.

Can I migrate from Heroku to Render?

Yes. Most apps migrate in < 1 hour. You'll need to: update your deploy settings, move environment variables, and change your database connection string.

Does Render have better uptime than Heroku?

Both have 99.9%+ uptime SLA. Uptime is comparable. Choose based on pricing and features.

Is Render good for production?

Yes. Render powers production apps for thousands of companies. At $7-50/month, it's much more affordable than Heroku while maintaining reliability.
