Semrush Pricing History (2020–2026)

All plans raised ~17% — inflation-driven increase January 2024
Semrush raised prices across all plans by approximately 17% in January 2024. Pro jumped from $119.95 to $139.95/month, Guru from $229.95 to $249.95/month, and Business from $449.95 to $499.95/month. Semrush cited "investment in AI features, database expansion, and inflation" in customer communications. The per-user add-on prices increased proportionally. Annual subscribers were grandfathered until their next renewal date.

Semrush added AI-powered writing tools, ContentShake AI, and expanded its Social Media Toolkit. Semrush also raised prices in select markets outside North America by 10-20% in Q3 2023 due to currency effects.

Semrush vs Ahrefs: Which Is Cheaper in 2026?

Semrush and Ahrefs are the two dominant all-in-one SEO platforms, and their pricing has diverged since 2021:

Ahrefs Lite ($129/mo monthly): Slightly cheaper than Semrush Pro ($139.95/mo), with similar core features — keyword research, backlink analysis, rank tracking. Ahrefs is generally preferred by link builders; Semrush by content marketers and agencies with its deeper content tools. See our Ahrefs pricing history.

Bottom line: For solo SEOs and small teams, Ahrefs Lite is marginally cheaper. For agencies needing white-label reports, content marketing tools, and multi-client management, Semrush Guru justifies its higher price. Both tools raised prices in 2023-2024.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cheapest way to use Semrush in 2026?

Annual billing on the Pro plan ($117.33/month billed annually = $1,407.96/year) is the cheapest entry point for full Semrush access. Semrush occasionally offers promotional pricing (7-day trials, limited-time discounts) through affiliates. There's no free tier beyond a limited free account with severely restricted data.

Can I share a Semrush account with my team?

Yes, but with limits. Each plan allows one user by default. Additional users cost approximately $45/user/month on Pro/Guru plans. Sharing login credentials violates Semrush's ToS. For agencies or teams, Semrush Agency plans offer better per-seat economics with manager access and white-label features.

Is Semrush worth $139.95/month for a solo SEO?

At $140/month for a solo practitioner, Semrush Pro is expensive if you're only using basic features. The value calculation depends on your use case: if Semrush helps you win or retain one $200+/month client, it pays for itself. Alternatives worth considering at lower price points: Ubersuggest (~$29/mo), Mangools (~$49/mo), or Ahrefs Lite ($129/mo for similar core features).

Does Semrush offer nonprofit or educational discounts?

Semrush offers academic accounts for students through partnerships with specific institutions. There's no publicly advertised nonprofit discount, but contacting their sales team may yield custom pricing for educational organizations.
