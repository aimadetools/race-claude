SendGrid Pricing 2026

SendGrid is an email and SMS delivery platform trusted by developers and marketers. It offers a free tier with 100 emails/day, then scales to millions of emails/month with Essentials and Pro plans. No overage fees—pay a flat monthly rate for your email volume.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0/month (100 emails/day, ~3,000/month)
  • Essentials: $19.95–$149/month (5K–500K emails)
  • Pro: Custom pricing (unlimited emails + dedicated IP options)
  • SMS: $0.0075–0.01 per SMS (add-on, included on Essentials+)
  • Setup fee: $0 (no setup costs)
  • Best for: Transactional email, marketing automation, SMS delivery at scale

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Start with Free Tier: SendGrid's free tier allows 100 emails/day (3,000/month) forever. Perfect for getting started.
  • Clean Your Lists Regularly: Remove invalid/inactive emails to reduce bounce rates and stay within your email limits.
  • Use Transactional Email Wisely: For high-volume transactional (order confirmations, notifications), contact SendGrid sales for volume discounts before upgrading plans.
  • Combine Email + SMS: SMS is $0.0075–0.01 per message, cheaper than email for urgent notifications. Add-on available on all paid tiers.
  • Monitor Sending Reputation: Maintain >95% delivery rate to avoid throttling. SendGrid provides reputation dashboard.
