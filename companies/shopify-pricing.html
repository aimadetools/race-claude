Shopify Pricing 2026 — Plans, Transaction Fees & What Changed

⚠️
January 2023: Shopify raised plan prices across the board — Basic from $29 to $39/mo (+34%), Shopify plan from $79 to $105/mo (+33%), Advanced from $299 to $399/mo (+33%). This was Shopify's first major price increase since its 2015 launch. Additional price adjustments followed in 2025.

Shopify Pricing History (2015–2026)

Shopify Plus minimum raised; Shopify Commerce Components introduced 2025
Shopify raised the Shopify Plus entry price from $2,000/month to $2,300/month. Simultaneously, Shopify introduced "Commerce Components" — a modular headless offering for enterprise retailers that can run $2,500–$10,000+/month depending on components selected. This effectively created a new pricing tier above Plus for large-scale merchants.
Shopify Plus minimum:$2,000/mo→$2,300/mo
All plans raised ~33–34% — first price increase since 2015 January 2023
Shopify's most significant pricing change in its history: all three core plans went up by roughly one-third simultaneously. Basic jumped from $29 to $39/month, the mid-tier Shopify plan from $79 to $105, and Advanced from $299 to $399. Shopify justified this with "inflation and investment in the platform." Merchants on annual plans were grandfathered for one year but eventually faced the increases on renewal.
Basic:$29/mo→$39/mo (+34%)
Shopify:$79/mo→$105/mo (+33%)
Advanced:$299/mo→$399/mo (+33%)
Transaction fees raised; Shopify Plus minimum doubled 2022
Shopify raised Shopify Plus minimum from $1,000/month to $2,000/month for new merchants (existing contracts honored). Non-Shopify Payments transaction fees were also quietly increased on some plans. This disproportionately affected enterprise merchants using third-party payment processors.
Shopify Plus minimum:$1,000/mo→$2,000/mo
Current pricing structure established 2015–2022
Shopify maintained its Basic ($29), Shopify ($79), and Advanced ($299) pricing for 8 years — an unusually long period of price stability. During this time, Shopify added significant features (online store 2.0, markets, B2B, checkout extensions) without raising core plan prices. This long stability made the 2023 increase more jarring for merchants who had budgeted around stable Shopify costs.

True Cost of Shopify: Plan + Transaction Fees

Shopify's transaction fees make the true cost highly dependent on revenue. If you use Shopify Payments, the transaction fees drop to zero (only standard card processing fees apply). If you use a third-party payment processor, the fees stack significantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest Shopify plan in 2026?

The Shopify Starter plan at $5/month is technically the cheapest, but it's limited to social selling and basic buy buttons — no full online store. For a real online store, Basic at $29/month (annual billing) is the entry point.

Does Shopify charge transaction fees in 2026?

Yes, if you use a payment processor other than Shopify Payments. Rates: 2% on Basic, 1% on Shopify plan, 0.5% on Advanced. If you use Shopify Payments (available in most countries), you pay zero transaction fees — only the standard card processing rate (2.4–2.9% + 30¢ depending on plan).

Is Shopify more expensive than WooCommerce?

WooCommerce itself is free software, but you pay separately for hosting ($5–30/month), domain, SSL, and plugins (which can add up quickly). Total cost of a WooCommerce store is typically $15–100/month depending on setup. Shopify is more expensive on paper but all-in-one — no hosting management, no plugin conflicts, and Shopify Payments eliminates external payment fees. For developers who want full control, WooCommerce wins on price. For merchants who want simplicity, Shopify's higher price is often worth it.

When does Shopify charge the annual vs monthly rate?

Annual plans are billed upfront for 12 months. The monthly rate shown for annual ($29 for Basic vs $39/month for monthly) is just the per-month equivalent — you pay the full year in advance. Most merchants on annual plans saw price increases kick in at their next renewal after January 2023.
