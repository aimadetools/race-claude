Shopify vs Etsy Pricing 2026

The creator's choice: Own your store with Shopify (full control, must build audience) or sell on Etsy's marketplace (built-in buyers, lower control)?

Key Price Comparison

  • Shopify Basic: $29/month + 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
  • Etsy: Free shop + $0.20 per listing (4-month duration) + 6.5% total fees (3% + 3% + $0.20)
  • At $2,000/mo sales: Shopify = $87/mo, Etsy = $150/mo (Shopify 42% cheaper)
  • Audience: Shopify = you build it; Etsy = 65+ million monthly shoppers (built-in traffic)
  • Best for: Shopify = brands, dropshipping, scaling; Etsy = handmade, vintage, artisans, quick sales

When to Choose Each

Choose Shopify If:

  • You're building a brand — Full control over store, design, messaging, and customer experience
  • You plan to scale significantly — Shopify becomes cheaper above $3,000/mo sales; Etsy margins shrink
  • You want to own customer relationships — Direct email/marketing access to buyers (Etsy restricts this)
  • You sell contemporary/new products — Shopify better for branded, manufactured goods; Etsy favors handmade/vintage
  • You want multi-channel presence — Sell on Shopify + Amazon + TikTok Shop simultaneously
  • You want lower fees long-term — Shopify 2.9% vs Etsy 6.5% (huge difference at scale)

Choose Etsy If:

  • You sell handmade, vintage, or unique items — Etsy buyers specifically search for artisan goods; Shopify requires you to drive traffic
  • You want immediate sales without marketing — Etsy's 65M+ monthly visitors = instant audience (Shopify = zero traffic until you market)
  • You're just starting out — No platform subscription ($0 vs $29/mo); only pay when you sell
  • You're not technical — Setup takes 15 minutes; Shopify requires design/customization knowledge
  • You want low customer acquisition cost — Etsy buyers are pre-qualified (they're already shopping there)
  • You value simplicity — Etsy handles everything; Shopify requires ongoing marketing effort

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I start on Etsy and move to Shopify later?

Yes! Many successful sellers start on Etsy (low risk, built-in audience) and migrate to Shopify once they have customer base and brand momentum. You'll have existing customer data to email directly on Shopify.

Do I need Etsy Ads to succeed on Etsy?

Not always. Etsy's search algorithm can drive organic traffic to well-optimized listings (good tags, titles, descriptions). Ads help, but aren't mandatory. On Shopify, external marketing (Google Ads, social) is usually required from the start.

What about selling on both Shopify AND Etsy?

Many sellers do this! Use Etsy for discoverability and Shopify for brand/margins. You'll need inventory sync tools, but it's possible. Splits your effort though.

How long does it take to build an audience on Shopify?

3–6 months of active marketing to see traction. Etsy can generate sales within weeks. If speed matters, Etsy wins. If long-term margins matter, Shopify wins.

Can I negotiate Etsy fees?

No. Etsy's fees are fixed across all sellers. Shopify's lower rates (2.9% vs Etsy's 6.5%) are a fundamental advantage for volume sellers.

What about Shopify's optional app costs?

Shopify apps (email, inventory, analytics) are optional but often needed. Estimate $50–200/mo in total app costs. Etsy has fewer integrations but simpler base platform.
