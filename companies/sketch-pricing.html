Why Choose Sketch?

  • Affordable: $120/year is 3-5x cheaper than Adobe Creative Cloud ($55/mo) and comparable to Figma ($144/year)
  • Mac-native: Optimized for macOS, faster performance on Mac than browser-based tools
  • Design systems: Sketch Libraries make managing design systems easier than Figma for some teams
  • No real-time co-editing limitation: Sketch Teams focuses on shared libraries and components, not simultaneous editing

When to Choose Figma vs Sketch

Choose Sketch if: You're a Mac-only design shop, prefer native performance over web, want affordability.

Choose Figma if: You have Windows users, need real-time co-editing, want more integrations, or prefer web-based tools. Figma's free tier is also more generous.

Both are great for: UI/UX design, prototyping, design systems. The choice comes down to platform requirements and team workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sketch really $120/year?

Yes. Sketch Individual is $120/year with annual billing (20% discount from $12.50/mo month-to-month). This makes it one of the most affordable professional design tools, cheaper than Figma's annual subscription ($144/year for Professional).

What's included in Sketch Individual?

All the core design features: artboards, vector drawing, components, libraries, prototyping, design systems, Cloud Sync (automatic backup), and asset libraries. The main limitation vs Team plans is no real-time collaboration — you don't get simultaneous editing.

Can I try Sketch free?

Yes, Sketch offers a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. After 30 days, you'll need to purchase a license.

Is Sketch worth it compared to free alternatives?

If you're a professional designer, yes. Sketch is less than $1/month on annual billing. Free tools like Figma's free tier or Penpot are good for learning, but lack some professional features. At $120/year, Sketch is a solid investment for indie designers and small agencies.

Do I need a Team workspace?

Only if you're collaborating with other designers on a shared design system. Sketch Teams ($20/person/month = $240/year per person) is aimed at design teams that need shared libraries and version control. Solo designers should use Individual ($120/year).
