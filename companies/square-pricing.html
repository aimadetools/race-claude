Square Products & Additional Costs

  • Square Online: Free basic store (no transaction limit). Plus plan $20/mo (email/SMS marketing, advanced inventory, reviews).
  • Square Point of Sale: Free to download. Hardware: Terminal (no cost, pay-as-you-go), Stand ($49), Reader ($29 one-time).
  • Square Invoices: Free to send. Customers pay 2.9% + $0.30 if paying by card.
  • Square Payroll: $35/month for first user, $10/mo per additional user. Includes payroll processing, tax filings, 1099 handling.
  • Square Cash Advance: Borrow up to $250K against future revenue. 5–8% of revenue repaid (interest-free).
