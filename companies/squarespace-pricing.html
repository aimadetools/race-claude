Squarespace Pricing 2026 — All Plans, Annual vs Monthly

📊
Squarespace 2026: Paid plans start at $16/month (annual). E-commerce without transaction fees starts at $27/month on Commerce Basic. No free plan — 14-day trial available. Annual billing saves ~30%.

Current Squarespace Pricing (2026)

Squarespace offers four paid plans. Prices shown are billed annually (save ~30% vs monthly). All plans include SSL, unlimited bandwidth, and a free custom domain for the first year.

Plan Annual Billing Monthly Billing Best For
Personal $16/month $23/month Personal sites, blogs, portfolios
Business $23/month $33/month Small business (3% transaction fee applies)
Commerce Basic $27/month $36/month Online stores, no transaction fees
Commerce Advanced $49/month $65/month Subscriptions, advanced shipping, abandoned cart

⚠️ The Business plan charges a 3% transaction fee on sales. Upgrade to Commerce Basic to remove this fee — it often pays for itself on the first $300 in monthly sales.

Real-World Cost Scenarios

Personal portfolio or blog (Personal plan)
Annual billing $192/year ($16 × 12)
Monthly billing $276/year ($23 × 12)
Annual savings $84/year

Personal includes unlimited pages, gallery pages, and 30-minute video storage. No e-commerce. Best for creative portfolios and personal blogs.

Online store — low volume (Commerce Basic)
Annual billing $324/year ($27 × 12)
Monthly billing $432/year ($36 × 12)
No transaction fees (vs Business plan on $1K/mo sales) Save $30/month in fees

Commerce Basic is better than Business if you sell more than $333/month — the 3% fee savings exceed the $4/month plan difference.

Growing store with subscriptions (Commerce Advanced)
Annual billing $588/year ($49 × 12)
Monthly billing $780/year ($65 × 12)
Annual savings $192/year

Commerce Advanced adds sell subscriptions, advanced discounts, abandoned cart recovery, and commerce APIs. Ideal for DTC brands and membership sites.
