Stripe vs Square Pricing 2026

The two leading payment processors battle it out on fees, features, and platform pricing.

Key Price Comparison

  • Stripe Online: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
  • Square Online: 2.6% + $0.30 per transaction
  • Winner: Square is ~0.3% cheaper per transaction
  • Best for: Stripe = SaaS/subscriptions; Square = retail/physical

Platform & Subscription Services

Stripe Billing (Subscriptions)

  • Setup: Free
  • Per-subscription fee: None (fees only on transactions)
  • Best for: SaaS, memberships, recurring billing with complex requirements
  • Usage: Included in Stripe account

Square Subscriptions

  • Setup: Free (also integrated into Square Online)
  • Per-subscription fee: None (fees only on transactions)
  • Best for: Small businesses, recurring payments, retail subscriptions
  • Usage: Included with Square account

Stripe Connect (Multi-Tenant)

  • Use case: Marketplace, escrow, splitting payments
  • Cost: Standard Stripe fees on each connected account
  • Best for: Complex platforms needing split payments

Who Should Choose Stripe?

  • SaaS companies — Subscription billing, metering, advanced use cases
  • Developer-first — Comprehensive API, best documentation, extensive webhooks
  • Complex workflows — Marketplace payments, splits, escrow, Stripe Connect
  • High-volume online — 2.6% in-person rate ties Square; online is competitive
  • Custom checkout — Highly customizable payment flows and UX

Who Should Choose Square?

  • Retail/restaurants — Full POS system, in-person is their bread and butter
  • Lower online fees — 2.6% online rate beats Stripe's 2.9% for high-volume
  • Small business simplicity — Simple interface, less code required
  • Omnichannel — Integrated online + in-person + inventory (all in one app)
  • International expansion — Better multi-currency support (200+ vs Stripe's 135)

Pricing History & Changes

  • Stripe: Fees stable since 2023. Last major change was 2021 when introducing Stripe Connect pricing. ACH fees introduced in 2020.
  • Square: Reduced online transaction fees from 2.9% to 2.6% in 2021 (aggressive move to gain market share). Rates stable since.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which processor is actually cheaper for my business?

It depends on your mix:

  • Online-only: Square saves 0.3% per transaction (~$36/year on $100K volume)
  • In-person + Online: Tied (both 2.6% in-person)
  • ACH transfers: Stripe is cheaper ($0.25 vs $1.00 per existing customer transfer)
  • Subscriptions: Stripe has better tools; fees are the same

Can I switch from one to the other easily?

Yes, but it takes planning. You'll need to:

  • Export transaction history (easy)
  • Migrate active subscriptions (Stripe has better tools for this)
  • Update API integrations (moderate work if custom)
  • Test in staging first (important)
Most migrations take 2-4 weeks.

Do they charge for chargebacks or disputes?

Both charge dispute fees (~$15). This is standard industry practice. The key difference: Stripe's tools for preventing chargebacks are better (3D Secure, radar automation).

What about ACH vs card fees?

Stripe ACH is 0.8% + $0.30 (much cheaper). Square ACH is 1% + $0.30. If you process lots of ACH (invoices, B2B), Stripe wins significantly.

Which has better fraud detection?

Stripe's Radar (AI-powered fraud detection) is industry-leading. Square's fraud detection is good but less sophisticated. If you're high-risk (digital goods, high-value), Stripe is safer.
