When to Choose Each Provider

✓ Choose Stripe if:

  • You're building SaaS with recurring billing
  • You need excellent API documentation and developer support
  • You operate internationally (100+ countries supported)
  • You need advanced features like Connect (marketplace payments)

Best for: SaaS, platforms, global businesses, developers

✓ Choose Square if:

  • You have in-person POS needs (Square Reader is industry-leading)
  • You run a retail, restaurant, or service business
  • You're in US, Canada, Australia, Japan, or Singapore
  • You want simple invoicing + POS in one platform

Best for: Retail, restaurants, local services, small businesses

✓ Choose PayPal if:

  • You want to accept PayPal, credit cards, and ACH in one account
  • You operate internationally but want a simpler platform
  • You already have established PayPal business presence
  • You want invoice + subscription support without custom APIs

Best for: Small businesses, invoicing-heavy workflows, non-tech teams

Bottom Line

For most SaaS/tech: Stripe. Best API, most flexible for subscriptions, best international support.

For retail/POS: Square. Industry-leading hardware (Reader), simplicity, great invoicing.

For simplicity: PayPal. All-in-one, no tech debt, widely trusted.

Cost difference: At small transaction volumes (<$10K/mo), Square saves ~5% vs Stripe/PayPal. Difference diminishes at scale.
