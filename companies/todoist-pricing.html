Todoist Pricing 2026

Todoist is a simple, powerful task management and productivity platform for individuals and teams. With a free plan that covers the basics and Pro at just $4/month, Todoist is one of the most affordable task managers available. Business plans at $6/user/month scale for larger teams.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0/month (unlimited tasks, basic organization, mobile apps)
  • Pro: $4/month (annual) / $5/month (monthly) (templates, recurring tasks, 50 file uploads)
  • Business: $6/user/month (annual) / $6/user/month (monthly) (team management, member invites, team insights)
  • Setup fee: $0 (no setup costs)
  • Annual Discount: Save on annual billing vs. monthly
  • Best for: Task management for individuals, small teams, freelancers

Todoist Features by Plan

Free Plan Includes:

  • Unlimited tasks (no limit)
  • Up to 3 projects
  • Mobile apps (iOS/Android)
  • Email support
  • Basic task organization (lists, subtasks)

Pro Plan Adds:

  • Unlimited projects
  • Project templates (30+ templates)
  • Recurring tasks
  • Labels (color-coded organization)
  • Advanced filters and search
  • 50 file uploads per project
  • Custom reminders
  • Priority levels

Business Plan Adds:

  • Team member management
  • Invite and manage team members
  • Team insights and analytics
  • Admin controls
  • Shared team projects
  • Priority support

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Start with Free: Todoist's free plan is surprisingly capable. Use it until you need unlimited projects or templates.
  • Pro is Unbeatable: At $4/month annual ($48/year), Todoist Pro is one of the cheapest productivity tools available.
  • Annual Billing: Pay annually to save ~17% compared to monthly billing ($4/mo vs $5/mo).
  • Business Plan at Scale: For teams of 5+, Todoist Business at $6/user/month is significantly cheaper than Asana ($10/user) or Monday.com ($12/user).
  • Use Templates: Pro plan comes with 30+ project templates. Use them to set up projects faster.
  • Integrate with Other Tools: Todoist integrates with Gmail, Slack, Zapier. Use Zapier to automate task creation from other apps.
