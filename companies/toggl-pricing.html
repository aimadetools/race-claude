Toggl Pricing 2026

Toggl is a time tracking and project management tool with a generous free tier for solo users and affordable per-user pricing for teams. Includes invoicing, project management, and integrations.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0 (unlimited projects, 1 user, invoicing included, basic reports)
  • Starter: $9/user/month (unlimited projects, basic reports, 1 project manager)
  • Premium: $18/user/month (unlimited project managers, advanced reports, integrations, API)
  • Team discount: Yes (5+ users get 15-20% discount, annual billing)
  • Best for: Freelancers, agencies, and small to mid-size teams tracking billable hours

Why Choose Toggl?

  • Free tier for solo work: Unlimited projects and invoicing on free plan. No upgrade needed until you add team members.
  • Easy invoicing: Built-in invoicing from time entries. Track billable hours, set rates per client/project, send invoices directly from Toggl.
  • Project management: Organize time tracking by client and project. Assign team members to projects with different permissions.
  • Mobile apps: Excellent iOS and Android apps with offline time tracking. Works great for remote teams.
  • Affordable for teams: At 5+ users, Toggl is competitive with Harvest and cheaper than some alternatives with team discounts.
  • Integrations: Connects with Zapier, Jira, Slack, Google Calendar, and more (Premium only).
