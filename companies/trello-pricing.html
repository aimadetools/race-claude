Trello Pricing 2026

Trello is a popular visual task management platform using kanban boards for organizing work. Pricing is per-user: free plan for unlimited personal use, Standard at $6/user/month for teams, and Premium at $12.50/user/month for advanced features and admin tools.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • Free: $0/month (unlimited personal boards, 1 group board)
  • Standard: $6/user/month (calendar view, advanced power-ups, file attachments)
  • Premium: $12.50/user/month (board templates, advanced automation, admin features)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (SSO, priority support, advanced security)
  • Annual Discount: ~15-20% off when paying yearly
  • Best for: Small to mid-size teams, visual task management, kanban workflows

Trello Features by Plan

Free Plan Includes:

  • Unlimited cards and lists
  • Basic board sharing and collaboration
  • Up to 10 MB file attachments
  • Basic power-ups (1 power-up per board)
  • Mobile app access

Standard Plan Adds:

  • Calendar and timeline views
  • Custom fields on cards
  • Unlimited power-ups
  • 250 MB file uploads
  • Card aging and board limits management

Premium Plan Adds:

  • Board templates library
  • Advanced automation rules
  • Admin console for team management
  • 1 GB file uploads
  • Priority email support

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Start with Free: Trello's free plan is generous for small teams. Use it until you need advanced features.
  • Standard for Most Teams: $6/user/month is the sweet spot. Most teams don't need Premium features.
  • Pay Annually: Save 15-20% by switching to annual billing instead of monthly.
  • Only Add Users to Paid Plans: Keep your free group board but only pay Standard/Premium for active team members.
  • Consider Basecamp Alternative: For 15+ users, Basecamp's $99/month flat rate may be cheaper than Trello's per-user pricing.
  • Use Power-Ups Wisely: Free plan users get 1 power-up per board. Standard/Premium users get unlimited—evaluate before upgrading.
