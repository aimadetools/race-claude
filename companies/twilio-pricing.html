Twilio Pricing 2026

Twilio is a pay-as-you-go communications platform offering SMS, voice calling, video, and messaging APIs. No monthly base fee—you pay for each SMS sent and minute of voice/video used.

Quick Pricing Summary

  • SMS: $0.0075 per outbound SMS (US), $0.01–0.20+ international
  • Voice calling: $0.0136 per minute (US), varies by country internationally
  • Video: $0.015 per minute per participant
  • WhatsApp messages: $0.005–0.10 depending on template/region
  • Base fee: $0 (no monthly minimum)
  • Best for: Startups and apps needing SMS/voice APIs with variable volume

Cost Optimization Tips

  • Enable Spending Alerts: Set monthly budgets in the Twilio console to catch unexpected spikes.
  • Use Smart Retry Logic: Implement exponential backoff for transient failures to avoid duplicate message sends.
  • Regional SMS Routes: International SMS costs vary widely. Use local carriers via Twilio for better rates in high-volume regions.
  • Batch Video Sessions: Group video participants to minimize per-participant-minute costs.
  • Consider Volume Discounts: Contact Twilio sales for volume pricing on 1M+ SMS/month.
