Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zapier still free in 2026?

Yes, but the free plan is significantly limited. After March 2026, it allows only 3 active Zaps (down from 5) and 100 tasks/month. For most automation use cases beyond basic personal tasks, a paid plan is needed.

What's the best Zapier alternative in 2026?

Popular alternatives include: Make (Integromat) — more powerful, cheaper, steeper learning curve; n8n — open source, self-hostable, free for self-hosted; Pipedream — developer-focused, generous free tier; Workato — enterprise-grade; and native integrations in tools like HubSpot, Notion, and Linear that reduce the need for middleware.

How many tasks does Zapier Professional include?

After March 2026, Zapier Professional includes 2,000 tasks/month at $59.99/month (annual). If you need more tasks, each additional 1,000 tasks costs extra, or you upgrade to Team ($103.50/month) for 50,000 tasks.

Does Zapier charge per Zap or per task?

Zapier charges per task — each action performed by a Zap counts as one task. A multi-step Zap that does 3 actions will use 3 tasks per trigger. Zapier counts tasks by actions, not by Zap runs, which makes usage harder to predict.
