When to Choose Each

Choose Zapier if:

  • You need 6,000+ app integrations (Zapier's ecosystem is broader)
  • Your team is non-technical and wants the easiest experience
  • You have fewer than 5 automations (free/Starter is enough)

Choose Make.com if:

  • Budget matters (Make is 81% cheaper at scale)
  • You need complex workflows with loops and conditional logic
  • You have 10+ automations (where Make pulls away on price)
  • Your required apps are in Make's 1,000+ (Gmail, Slack, HubSpot, etc.)
