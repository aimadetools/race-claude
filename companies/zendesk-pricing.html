Zendesk Pricing History (2024–2026)

Zendesk has steadily increased pricing every 12-18 months. The company has consolidated products and created a clearer tier structure, but per-agent costs have consistently risen.

Team Edition & Professional price increase November 2025
Zendesk raised pricing on Team Edition (+12%) and Professional (+11%) in Q4 2025. The pricing increase was communicated via email 30 days in advance. Existing customers retained their old pricing through their renewal date, then auto-upgraded to new rates.
Team Edition:$49/agent/mo→$55/agent/mo (+12%)
Professional:$89/agent/mo→$99/agent/mo (+11%)
Enterprise & Enterprise Plus pricing established June 2025
Zendesk clarified its premium tier structure. The old "Premium" and "Enterprise" tiers were renamed to "Professional" and "Enterprise," with a new "Enterprise Plus" tier at the top. Custom add-ons (advanced analytics, priority support) were bundled into tiers rather than sold separately.
Premium → Professional:$89/agent/mo (unchanged)→Same rate, renamed
Enterprise:$149/agent/mo→$199/agent/mo (+34%)
Limits added to Team Edition February 2025
Zendesk added explicit limits to the Team Edition tier: max 3 users per team (previously unlimited), 500 tickets/month per user (previously unlimited), and restricted API access. These limits pushed many customers toward Professional tier.
Team Edition users:Unlimited→Max 3 users
Team Edition tickets:Unlimited→500 tickets/month
Price increase across all tiers August 2024
Zendesk raised pricing in August 2024, with Team Edition going from $45 to $49/agent/mo. This set the stage for the November 2025 increase. Most customers received 30 days notice via email, but the increase was enforced at renewal regardless of current contract terms.
Team Edition:$45/agent/mo→$49/agent/mo (+9%)

What Zendesk's Price Increases Mean for Teams

A 5-agent support team on Team Edition paid $245/month ($2,940/year) in August 2024. In April 2026, that same team pays $275/month ($3,300/year) — a $360/year increase (12%) in just 20 months. Multiply this by 10 agents, and you're looking at a $720/year increase.

For scaling support teams, Zendesk's limits on lower tiers force upgrades to Professional, which now costs $99/agent/month. A team that could stay on Team Edition in 2023 must now move to Professional in 2026 — adding $2,400/year in costs for a 10-agent team.

Zendesk's competitors (Freshdesk, Gorgias, Help Scout) are watching these price increases carefully. If you're evaluating helpdesk platforms, factor in annual price increases of 8-12% when calculating your 3-year total cost of ownership.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Zendesk cost for a small team?

For a 5-agent support team on Team Edition (as of April 2026), the cost is $275/month or $3,300/year on monthly billing. Professional tier costs $495/month ($5,940/year). Most organizations save 10-15% by paying annually.

What's the difference between Team Edition and Professional?

Team Edition ($55/agent/mo) includes basic ticketing, limited automation, and agent limits. Professional ($99/agent/mo) adds advanced automation, SLAs, conditional routing, custom fields, and unlimited agents. Team Edition is capped at 3 users per team as of February 2025.

Does Zendesk offer annual discounts?

Yes, Zendesk typically offers 10-15% discounts for annual prepayment. Some enterprise customers negotiate 20-25% discounts for multi-year contracts. Contact Zendesk sales for a custom quote if you have 10+ agents.

How does Zendesk compare to Freshdesk?

Freshdesk Starter tier is $15/agent/month (vs. Zendesk Team Edition at $55). Freshdesk's Pro tier is $49/agent/month (vs. Zendesk Professional at $99). For small support teams, Freshdesk is dramatically cheaper. Zendesk's advantage is in advanced customization and third-party integrations for large enterprises.

When will Zendesk raise prices again?

Based on Zendesk's pattern (increases in Aug 2024, Nov 2025), the next increase is likely in Q4 2026 or Q1 2027. The most reliable way to prepare is to set up a pricing monitor that alerts you within hours of any pricing page change.
