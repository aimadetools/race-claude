Cost Breakdown: 10-Agent Support Team

Zendesk Suite Team — 10 Agents

Annual billing: 10 × $55/mo × 12 months

Total: $6,600/year

Includes email, chat, voice. No additional omnichannel add-ons needed for basic support. But AI features are a separate add-on at $50+/agent/mo.

Freshdesk Growth — 10 Agents

Annual billing: 10 × $15/mo × 12 months

Total: $1,800/year

Savings vs Zendesk Suite Team: $4,800/year. If you need voice support, add Freshcaller at ~$19/agent/mo (adds $2,280/year). Even with voice: $4,080/year — still 38% cheaper than Zendesk alone.

Cost at 50 Agents: Full Enterprise

Freshdesk Enterprise (50 agents): 50 × $79 × 12 = $47,400/year

Freshdesk saves $42,000/year at 50 agents. For large support teams, Freshdesk's cost advantage is significant — nearly $3.5M in savings over 10 years.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose Zendesk if: You need enterprise-grade customization and flexibility. You want all channels (email, chat, voice, social) natively in one subscription. Your team has complex routing and workflow requirements. You need best-in-class reporting and analytics. You're a larger company where support infrastructure reliability is paramount.

Choose Freshdesk if: You're cost-sensitive and want maximum value per dollar. You want a free tier to start (Zendesk has no free plan). You're a startup or SMB that doesn't need Zendesk's full enterprise feature set. You want a simpler setup with less configuration overhead. You can tolerate add-ons for voice/chat (Freshcaller/Freshchat).

Key insight: Zendesk's 2022 PE acquisition and subsequent price increases have pushed many SMBs toward Freshdesk. For teams under 50 agents, Freshdesk offers excellent value with most of the features growing companies need. The gap widens at Enterprise level.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Freshdesk or Zendesk cheaper?

Freshdesk is significantly cheaper. Freshdesk Growth at $15/agent/mo vs Zendesk Suite Team at $55/agent/mo — Freshdesk is 73% less expensive at comparable tiers. Freshdesk also has a free plan for unlimited agents; Zendesk has no free plan since 2023.

Did Zendesk raise prices?

Yes. After being taken private by a PE consortium in 2022, Zendesk discontinued its free Lite plan in 2023 and has raised pricing multiple times. Suite Team went from $49 to $55/agent/mo in 2026, a 12% increase. Enterprise plans saw larger increases of 15–20%.

Is Freshdesk good for large enterprises?

Yes, Freshdesk Enterprise at $79/agent/mo includes audit logs, custom roles, sandbox environments, and IP whitelisting — comparable to Zendesk Suite Professional ($115/agent/mo). For most enterprise requirements, Freshdesk is a capable alternative at a 30% lower price point.

What's missing from Freshdesk compared to Zendesk?

Freshdesk's main gaps vs Zendesk: voice and live chat are separate add-ons (not included in base plans), reporting is less powerful on lower tiers, and the platform has fewer customization options for complex enterprise workflows. For straightforward help desk needs, Freshdesk covers most bases.

Can I migrate from Zendesk to Freshdesk?

Yes. Freshdesk offers migration tools and services for Zendesk customers. Ticket history, customer data, and knowledge base articles can be imported. Migration complexity varies with customization depth — simple setups can migrate in hours, complex ones may take days. Freshdesk sales teams often offer migration incentives.
