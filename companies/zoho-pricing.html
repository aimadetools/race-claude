Zoho CRM Pricing 2026 — Plans, Costs & Zoho One

📊
Zoho CRM 2026: Free plan supports 3 users with no time limit. Paid plans start at $14/user/month (annual). Zoho One — all 45+ Zoho apps — is $37/user/month and often the best deal for growing teams.

Current Zoho CRM Pricing (2026)

Zoho CRM uses per-user, per-month pricing with significant discounts for annual billing. All plans include unlimited contacts and deals.

Is Zoho One Worth It?

Zoho One bundles 45+ Zoho applications — CRM, Books (accounting), Projects, Desk (support), Campaigns (email marketing), Analytics, Sign, and more — for $37/user/month on annual all-employee pricing.

It's worth it if your team uses (or would use) more than 2-3 Zoho apps. Buying CRM Enterprise ($40/user) + Zoho Books ($15/user) alone costs more than Zoho One. For teams already invested in the Zoho ecosystem, Zoho One is almost always the right choice.

Zoho One vs Individual Apps (10 users)
CRM Enterprise alone $4,800/year
CRM + Books + Campaigns ~$9,600/year
Zoho One (45+ apps) $4,440/year

Zoho One is cheaper than CRM Enterprise alone at 10 users — and includes 44 additional apps.
