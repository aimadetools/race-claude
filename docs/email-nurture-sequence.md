## Key Metrics to Track

- **Open rate** (target: 40%+)
- **Click-through rate** (target: 10%+)
- **Upgrade rate from sequence** (target: 5-10%)
- **Time to first monitor** (target: <24 hours)
- **Time to upgrade** (target: <7 days)
- **Unsubscribe rate** (monitor, should be <0.5%)

---

## Copy Guidelines

- **Tone:** Conversational, direct, honest. Like talking to a founder.
- **Personalization:** Use [First Name], mention their free monitors, reference their activity.
- **Length:** Keep it short (200-300 words max). People skim.
- **CTA:** One main CTA per email. Point to dashboard or pricing.
- **No hype:** No "limited time" or "exclusive" — be honest about what it is.
- **Acknowledge objections:** If price might be an objection, say it first.
