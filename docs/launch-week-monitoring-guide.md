## DECISION TREE: What to Do If Day 1 Metrics Are Off

### **Scenario 1: Show IH Got ZERO Upvotes in First 2 Hours**

**Probable cause:**
- Account too new (need 1+ week history)
- Post title/formatting issue
- Post didn't appear in feed (rare)

**Action:**
- [ ] Check: Can you see your post on the IH homepage? (Not just in profile)
- [ ] If NOT visible: Delete and repost, or switch to "Ask IH" category
- [ ] If visible: Engage in other IH posts to build credibility (comment on 5 popular posts)
- [ ] **Don't rely on IH.** Pivot to Twitter + cold email immediately
- [ ] Set up Plan B: Post on Twitter at 6 PM with Show IH link

### **Scenario 2: Show IH Getting Upvotes But ZERO Signups**

**Probable cause:**
- Link clicks not converting to signups (site loading slow?)
- Wrong audience (IH users don't value your problem)
- Copy mismatch (IH title promising something signup doesn't deliver)

**Action:**
- [ ] Test signup flow yourself (try signing up from incognito window)
- [ ] Check: Does homepage match IH post title promise?
- [ ] Check admin.html: Do you see ANY signups from other sources? (If yes = site works, IH issue)
- [ ] If site works but IH converts at 0%: Switch budget to Twitter + cold email
- [ ] Reply to Show IH comments with direct signup link (or "reply with email" to gather feedback)

### **Scenario 3: LOTS of Show IH Upvotes But LOTS of Negative Comments**

**Probable cause:**
- Pricing too high
- Feature claims misunderstood
- Competitor comparison too aggressive
- Users see it as premature/incomplete

**Action:**
- [ ] Read comments carefully — what's the specific objection?
- [ ] If pricing objection: Highlight "free tier exists" + "detect 1 company free"
- [ ] If feature objection: Be honest ("this is v1, coming soon" is better than over-promising)
- [ ] If "premature launch" feedback: Lean into it ("Building publicly, early access available")
- [ ] Delete spam/troll comments. Engage thoughtfully with legit criticism.
- [ ] Don't change product for Day 1 feedback. Collect it, decide after Week 1.

### **Scenario 4: Signups Are Coming But ZERO Paid Conversions**

**Probable cause:**
- Free tier is so good they don't need to pay
- Pricing page not compelling
- Trust issues (new product, new brand)
- Feature gap (they need something only Pro has, but it's unclear)

**Action:**
- [ ] Check: Are free users seeing the upgrade prompt in dashboard?
- [ ] Check: Do they hit the 5-monitor limit quickly? (If not, limits are too generous)
- [ ] Check show-ih-response-guide.md: Reply to "Is it worth $19?" comments with specific answers
- [ ] Don't cut pricing yet. Collect feedback for Day 3 decision.
- [ ] A/B test: Some free users should hit limit at 5 monitors (default), some at 10 (experiment)

### **Scenario 5: Cold Email Batch 1 Got 0 Replies**

**Probable cause:**
- List quality (wrong personas, invalid emails)
- Subject line weak (low open rate)
- Landing page mismatch (they click but don't convert)
- Bad timing (sent too late/too early)

**Action:**
- [ ] Check: Any bounces? If >5%, fix list quality
- [ ] Check: Any opens but no clicks? If yes = subject works, body or CTA issue
- [ ] Check: Clicks but no signups? = landing page issue (test signup flow)
- [ ] Batch 2: Try new subject line + check open rates in email client
- [ ] Send during morning (9 AM–12 PM) and mid-afternoon (2–4 PM) for next batch

## RED FLAGS — When to Pivot

### **Red Flag #1: Day 2 Signups Drop 50%+ from Day 1**
- Expected: Day 1 spike, then stabilization
- Unhealthy: Day 2 <10 signups if Day 1 was 30+
- Action: Show IH momentum died. Pivot to Twitter + cold email hard push.

### **Red Flag #2: Zero Paid Conversions After 50 Signups**
- Expected: 5–10 paid conversions per 50 signups
- Unhealthy: 0 conversions after 50 free signups
- Action: Feature gap or pricing issue. Check feedback. Offer free trial for early users.

### **Red Flag #3: High Show IH Engagement But 0 Signups**
- Expected: 30 IH upvotes = 5–10 signups
- Unhealthy: 50 IH upvotes = 0–1 signup
- Action: Link is broken, or landing page isn't compelling. Test signup flow. Recheck IH post link.

### **Red Flag #4: Cold Email 25%+ Bounce Rate**
- Expected: <2% bounce rate
- Unhealthy: >5% bounces
- Action: List quality issue. Verify emails before sending. Use Hunter.io or similar for validation.

### **Red Flag #5: All Signups From One Channel Only**
- Expected: Diversified across IH, Twitter, cold email
- Unhealthy: 100% from IH, 0 from other sources
- Action: Diversify immediately. Double down on cold email + Twitter to reduce dependency.

### **If Show IH Is Working (20+ upvotes, 5+ signups):**
- ✅ Keep engaging (reply to comments throughout the day)
- ✅ Bump cold email volume (send 20/day instead of 10/day)

- ❌ Don't keep pushing IH

- ✅ Consider posting on "Ask IH" or other communities instead

### **If Conversion Rate Is <5%:**
- ✅ Do competitive pricing check (are we too expensive?)
- ✅ Test free tier expansion (10 monitors instead of 5)
- ✅ Call 2-3 free users: Why didn't you upgrade?

### **If Conversion Rate Is >10%:**
- ✅ Raise prices (test $29/mo instead of $19)
- ✅ Increase cold email volume (we're doing great)
- ✅ Focus on retention (reduce churn below 5%)
