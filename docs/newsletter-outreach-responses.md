## Newsletter Performance Tracking

If/when newsletters feature the calculator, track:
- UTM source in visitor data: `utm_source=newsletter_name`
- Visitor count from each newsletter
- Conversion rate (visitors → signups)
- Email capture rate from calculator (how many use "Alert me when prices change")
- Signup-to-monitor rate (how many add at least 1 competitor)

Example: Newsletter sends 200 visitors → 30 create account (15%) → 15 add monitor (50%) = 15 new free tier users
