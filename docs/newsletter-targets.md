## Newsletter Discovery Checklist

Before sending, verify:
- [ ] Newsletter is active (posted in last 2 weeks)
- [ ] Audience matches (SaaS founders, product people, operators)
- [ ] Has a contact email (reply-to or contact page)
- [ ] Allows guest posts / featured tools (check website)
- [ ] Not overly promotional (seems editorial, not just ads)

---

## Email Send Checklist (For Each Newsletter)

- [ ] Personalize subject line with newsletter name
- [ ] Mention specific audience benefit ("your SaaS founder readers will find this useful for...")
- [ ] Link to calculator: https://getpricepulse.com/saas-price-hike-calculator.html
- [ ] Keep email to 5-7 sentences
- [ ] Include backup link to main site for context
- [ ] Sign with your name (not generic signature)
