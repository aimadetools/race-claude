## Executive Summary

Product Hunt is the #1 distribution channel for SaaS founders launching indie products. A strong PH launch can generate 300-500 signups, establish social proof, and drive organic search momentum. PricePulse targets a **Tuesday launch with 150+ upvotes, 50+ comments, and feature in Top 5 for the day**, resulting in 400-600 qualified signups and 40-60 paid conversions within 7 days.

**Launch goals:**
- #1-5 ranking on Product Hunt for the day
- 150+ upvotes
- 50+ comments (engagement)
- 400-600 signups (4:1 signup:customer ratio expected)
- 40-60 paid conversions within 30 days
- Organic ranking boost (backlinks from PH article)

## Pre-Launch Strategy (2 weeks before)

### Week 1: Community Building & Hunter Outreach

**Hunter Selection (Days 1-2)**
- Target 3-5 experienced hunters (300+ followers, 3+ successful launches)
- Criteria:
  - In SaaS/startup niche
  - Recent launches with 200+ upvotes
  - Engaged community (5+ comments per post)
- **Recommended hunters:**
  - Look at Stripe, Linear, Notion, Typeform launches
  - Use ProductHunt.com to filter by category + upvote count
- **Outreach email:** Personalized, mention why they'd be great fit, offer early access
- **Timeline:** Reach out 10 days before launch, confirm 7 days before

**PH Community Warm-Up (Days 3-7)**
- Create PH profile (if new) with polished profile pic, compelling bio
- Follow 50+ relevant product makers in pricing/SaaS niche
- Comment thoughtfully on 3-5 existing PH posts (real engagement, not spam)
- Build credibility before launch day
- Join #makers channel on PH Slack, introduce yourself

**Content Prep (Days 3-7)**
- Refine product tagline: "Competitor pricing tracker for indie founders" (short, compelling)
- Write 2-3 draft versions of PH post copy
- Create PH assets (see "Day-of Assets" section)
- Prepare first comment (10 FAQ answers written)

---

### Week 2: Final Prep & Soft Launch

**Assets Creation (Days 8-10)**
- **Product Hunt thumbnail:** 400×500 px, clean design with PricePulse logo
  - Use template: [ProductHunt asset kit](https://help.producthunt.com/en/articles/1838635-assets-required-for-launching)
  - Design: Dark theme matching brand, prominent antenna emoji 📡
- **Demo video:** 60-90 seconds max (how to set up a monitor, get an alert)
  - Show: Sign up → Add monitor → Real-time diff view
  - Upload to Vimeo, embed in PH post
- **Screenshots:** 2-3 key screenshots
  - Screenshot 1: Dashboard with sample monitors
  - Screenshot 2: Diff view highlighting pricing change
  - Screenshot 3: Alert email
- **First comment copy:** 10 anticipated FAQ answers (ready to post immediately)

**Soft Launch & Feedback (Days 11-13)**
- Share draft PH post with 5-10 early users/advisors
- Get feedback on copy, hook, CTA clarity
- Refine based on feedback
- Test all PH submission fields (link validation, etc.)

**Distribution List Prep (Days 11-14)**
- **Upvote list:** Create list of 100+ people likely to upvote
  - Existing waitlist members
  - Early access users (dashboard beta)
  - Twitter followers
  - Indie Hacker community
  - LinkedIn network
- **Comment list:** Identify 20+ people likely to engage in comments
  - SaaS founders
  - Pricing enthusiasts
  - Competitor tool users
- **Messaging templates:** Prepare DM/email templates for sharing
  - Morning launch: "Hey, we just launched on PH! Check it out and let me know what you think"
  - Afternoon reminder: "Last chance to vote if you like it 👍"

**Email & Social Prep (Days 11-14)**
- Draft email to waitlist (goes out 6 AM launch day)
  - Subject: "We launched! 🚀 Competitor pricing tracker for SaaS founders"
  - Body: Hook + feature list + PH link + CTA
  - Personalize: "You've been waiting 3 weeks for this"
- Draft Twitter thread announcing launch (thread ≈ 8-10 tweets)
  - Thread 1 (6 AM): "Today we launch PricePulse. Here's what 30 days monitoring revealed..."
  - Thread 2 (12 PM): "If you hate checking competitor pricing manually..."
  - Thread 3 (6 PM): "The best part? It costs $19/month."
- Draft LinkedIn post (professional, data-driven angle)
- Draft HN post (if applicable, post to "Show HN" after strong PH momentum)

## Launch Day Strategy (Tuesday, May 5)

### Morning (6 AM - 12 PM)

**6:00 AM — Go Live**
- Submit product to ProductHunt
- Use refined PH post copy:
  - Hook (problem): "Every SaaS founder has checked a competitor's pricing page last-minute"
  - Hook (solution): "PricePulse tells you instantly when they change it"
  - USP: "Built for indie founders, not enterprises. $19/month, not $500"
  - CTA: "Try free for 2 competitors" (clear, action-oriented)
- Post first comment immediately with FAQ answers
- Screenshot post live + celebrate (internal Slack)

**6:15 AM — Activate Distribution Channels**
- Send email to waitlist (subject: compelling, not spammy)
- Post Twitter thread (pin to profile)
- Post LinkedIn post
- Post to Indie Hackers (comments on related posts + Show IH discussion)
- Send DMs to close network (10-20 people)

**9 AM - 12 PM — Active Engagement**
- Monitor PH comments every 30 min
- Reply to EVERY comment within 30 minutes (shows responsiveness)
- Engage authentically (answer questions, don't sell)
- Share user wins in real-time ("Just got first 3 monitors set up!")
- Retweet/engage with early Twitter support

**12 PM — Midday Push**
- Post Twitter reminder tweet (afternoon push)
- Share to relevant communities:
  - SaaS communities (Slack groups, Discord)
  - Reddit: r/startups, r/SaaS, r/founder
  - LinkedIn posts (reach)
  - Hacker News (if strong momentum, post "Show HN")

---

### Afternoon (12 PM - 8 PM)

**1-3 PM — Momentum Maintenance**
- Check PH ranking (should be 20-100 by now)
- Continue responding to all comments (crucial for engagement)
- If questions come up repeatedly, add to first comment FAQ
- Share testimonial/social proof if any beta users review

**3-5 PM — Social Proof & FOMO**
- Post update on Twitter: "Wow, already 50+ people on PricePulse. Here's what they said..." (if true)
- Encourage people to share their own use cases
- Highlight compelling comments on PH: "Love this feedback →" (quote + reply)

**5-8 PM — End of Day Push**
- Post evening Twitter reminder
- Share to additional communities if not done
- Monitor and respond to comments
- Track metrics: upvotes, comments, click-through rate

---

### Evening/Night (8 PM - 12 AM)

**8 PM - 12 AM — International Expansion**
- Post in international SaaS communities (UK, EU timezone)
- Let organic momentum carry (don't over-push)
- Get rest — tomorrow is longer day

## Days 2-7 Post-Launch Momentum

### Day 2 (Wednesday)
- Continue commenting responsibly (don't spam)
- Respond to all new comments within 1 hour
- Share blog post: "Here's what 24 hours on Product Hunt taught us" (quick turnaround)
- Target: Maintain Top 20-30 ranking

### Days 3-4 (Thursday-Friday)
- By now, should be trending down from peak (normal)
- Focus shifts from upvote hunting to engagement quality
- Comment on related products, help other launches (karma)
- Track signups coming from PH (analytics tag: ?ref=producthunt)
- Share early wins: "First Starter customer! They're monitoring Notion + Stripe pricing"

### Days 5-7 (Weekend - Monday)
- PH momentum fades (normal, 7-day cycle)
- Shift focus to converting signups to paid
- Create email sequence for PH signups:
  - Day 1: "Thanks for signing up" (onboarding)
  - Day 3: "Check out these pricing trends" (value)
  - Day 7: "Upgrade to Starter for unlimited monitors" (ask)

## Contingency Plans

### If Launch Underperforms (<50 upvotes by 12 PM)
- **Action 1:** Check if PH post copy is compelling (reread hook)
- **Action 2:** Increase DM/email to distribution list (more aggressive outreach)
- **Action 3:** Check for technical issues (link working, product loadable)
- **Action 4:** Post on HN in parallel (mitigates single-channel risk)
- **Lesson:** Prepare backup channels (IH, HN, Twitter) to diversify risk

### If Comments Turn Negative ("Another pricing tracker?")
- **Action 1:** Acknowledge genuinely ("Valid point, here's what we do differently...")
- **Action 2:** Reference specific differentiation (pricing-aware diffs, indie pricing)
- **Action 3:** Ask for clarification ("What would make this valuable for you?")
- **Action 4:** Don't get defensive (kills engagement)

### If Technical Issues Occur (Site slow, signup broken)
- **Action 1:** Post immediately on PH: "We're getting crushed with traffic, fixing now"
- **Action 2:** Switch to Stripe checkout for signups (if main flow broken)
- **Action 3:** Be transparent about ETAs
- **Action 4:** Extend the moment after fix ("Thank you for patience, here's $20 credit")
