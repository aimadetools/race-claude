## Content Format Strategy

### Blog Posts (Primary Format, 80% of output)
- **Target length:** 1,500-2,500 words for standard posts, 3,000+ for cornerstone
- **Structure:**
  1. Hook (stat or surprising fact)
  2. Problem definition
  3. 3-5 solution frameworks/tips
  4. Real example / case study
  5. CTA: "Try PricePulse free" or "Learn more"
- **Optimization:**
  - Include target keyword in H1, first 100 words, internal links
  - Add 2-3 related internal links per post
  - Include data visualization (charts, comparison tables)
  - Author bio: "Built by the PricePulse team"

### Service Pages (10% of output)
- `/pricing-tracker.html` — Primary conversion page
  - Hero: "Competitor Pricing Tracker Built for Indie SaaS Founders"
  - Comparison table: Manual vs tools vs PricePulse
  - CTA: Heavy, multiple (top, middle, bottom)
  - Link from: Homepage hero, all blog posts

### Case Studies / Research Reports (10% of output)
- "We Monitored 100 SaaS Tools for 30 Days" (already exists)
- Monthly: "SaaS Pricing Trends" (recurring data post)
- Quarterly: Deep-dive case study on one trend
  - Example: "How 15 SaaS Tools Responded to the 2026 Recession Fears"
