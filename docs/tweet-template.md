## Notes

- Keep PricePulse mention at the end (not the hook)
- Emphasize founder pain (manual work, missing changes)
- Use specific numbers (34 out of 100, $19/mo, 24/7)
- Call to action should be simple: "Check it out" or "Let me know what you think"
- Reply to interesting conversations in SaaS/founder spaces
- Tag similar tools (Crayon, Visualping) only if making a direct comparison (sparingly)
