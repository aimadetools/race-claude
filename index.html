FAQ

Common questions

How does the monitoring actually work?

PricePulse fetches each competitor's pricing page on a regular schedule (daily on Free, hourly on Starter and Pro). We extract the meaningful text content, strip out ads and banners, and compare it against the previous snapshot. If anything meaningful changed, we alert you immediately.

What counts as a "change"?

We alert on changes to pricing text, plan names, feature descriptions, and CTAs. We filter out things like dates, cookie banners, and dynamic content that changes on every load. You won't get spammed with false positives.

Can it monitor JavaScript-rendered pages (React/Vue SPAs)?

It depends. PricePulse fetches the initial HTML response and uses CSS selector-based extraction to find pricing-relevant content. Most SaaS pricing pages include their key content in the initial HTML (even if styled with React/Vue), and those work well. Pages that require full JavaScript execution to render any content may not be reliably monitored. Full headless browser support is on the roadmap.

What if I hit my competitor limit?

You'll see a prompt in your dashboard when you hit your limit. You can remove old monitors or upgrade your plan. We never silently drop your monitors.

Is there a free trial?

The free plan is free forever — 2 competitors with daily checks. That's your trial. Try the product at no cost, and upgrade when you need more monitors or hourly checks. No credit card required to sign up.

What's your refund policy?

If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days of a paid plan, we'll refund you in full. No questions asked.
