Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you sign up for PricePulse, we collect:

  • Account data: Email address, name (optional), password hash
  • Monitor data: URLs you choose to monitor, monitor names, monitoring preferences
  • Usage data: Which competitors you monitor, when you access your dashboard, which alerts you view
  • Payment data: Handled by Stripe (we never see your card details)

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service: Monitor the URLs you specify and send you alerts
  • To send emails: Alert notifications, welcome email, occasional feature announcements
  • To improve PricePulse: Understand which features are used, fix bugs, make the product better
  • For billing: Process subscriptions via Stripe

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Supabase: User accounts, monitors, alerts, pricing snapshots (hosted on AWS US East)
  • Resend: Email delivery service for alerts and newsletters
  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI compliant)
  • VPS: Monitoring engine stores temporary copies of competitor pricing pages

Data Retention

  • Free plan: 7-day pricing history
  • Starter plan: 90-day pricing history
  • Pro plan: Unlimited pricing history
  • User account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Email logs: Retained for 1 year for delivery analytics

Your Rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion: You can request account deletion at any time (email hello@getpricepulse.com)
  • Unsubscribe: Every email has an unsubscribe link — use it anytime
  • Portability: You can request your monitor list and alert history

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in to your account
  • No tracking cookies: We don't use Google Analytics or other third-party trackers
  • No ads: We don't sell your data or serve ads

Security

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted)
  • Passwords are hashed and salted (never stored in plain text)
  • Database has row-level security (you can only see your own monitors)
  • API endpoints require authentication before processing

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we'll notify you via email or by posting prominently on our site.

Questions?

Email us at hello@getpricepulse.com
